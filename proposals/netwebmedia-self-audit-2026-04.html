Critical Findings (9 Fixes)

SEO Gaps

  • Meta description missing or wrong length. This is the single biggest organic-CTR lever. Costs you ~30% of potential clicks from search results.
  • No canonical URL. Exposes you to duplicate-content penalties across the www/non-www variants and query strings.
  • Missing Open Graph tags. When people share your link on LinkedIn/Slack/Twitter, the preview card is broken or blank — killing social CTR.
  • No Twitter Card tag. Same problem, Twitter-specific preview.
  • No JSON-LD structured data. AI answer engines (SGE, Perplexity, ChatGPT) can't confidently cite you. This is the #1 cause of invisibility in AEO.

Performance Wins You're Not Taking

  • Images not lazy-loaded (0 / 4). Simple loading="lazy" attribute would cut initial paint by 200-400ms on mobile.
  • Scripts not deferred (0 / 1). Blocks first paint unnecessarily.

Semantic HTML

  • Only 2 landmark elements. We expect 4 (header, nav, main, footer). Screen readers and LLM extractors need these to understand page structure.
