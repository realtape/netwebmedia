# NetWebMedia Templates

## Qualification checklist

- What service or product is being sold?
- What does a qualified lead look like?
- Which channels are already active?
- What is the average response time today?
- Is there a closer or sales owner in place?
- What budget is available for marketing and follow-up tooling?
- Which geography and language requirements matter?

## Proposal template structure

1. Client objective
2. Current bottleneck
3. Recommended growth system
4. Scope of setup phase
5. Scope of monthly retainer
6. KPI definitions
7. Timeline
8. Investment
9. Responsibilities and approvals

## Onboarding questionnaire

- Core offer description
- Target geography
- Ideal customer profile
- Current channels
- Current website or landing page
- CRM in use today
- Sales owner and contact method
- Required compliance or approval constraints

## KPI definition template

- Leads generated
- Qualified leads
- Booked calls
- Cost per lead
- Cost per qualified lead
- Landing page conversion rate
- Response time
- Close rate when available

## Monthly report template

### Executive summary

- What improved
- What underperformed
- Biggest constraint
- Recommended next move

### Channel and conversion review

- Traffic summary
- Conversion summary
- Lead quality notes

### Operational review

- Response time
- Missed lead risks
- Automation issues

### Next-month priorities

- Channel changes
- Page changes
- CRM or automation updates
