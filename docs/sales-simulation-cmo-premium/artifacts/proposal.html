1 · What we heard (discovery call, 4 May)

Test Premium Law Group is a 12-attorney firm across 4 cities. Five bottlenecks surfaced from the call & AEO audit:

  1. Intake is fractured across 4 offices. Each office handles inquiries differently. Conversion from inquiry to retainer varies 12% → 28%.
  2. AEO citation gaps in 5 of 6 practice areas. Audit showed firm appears in ChatGPT for only one practice area in one city — competitors dominate the rest.
  3. No senior-partner SDR coverage. Two senior partners want their own inbound lead pipeline they can hand off — currently they manage personally.
  4. Paid ads are off. No Google Ads. No Meta Ads. Competitors are spending $40k/mo combined in the target markets.
  5. Referral nurture and CLE content are siloed. No system connects past clients to referral asks or thought-leadership distribution.
