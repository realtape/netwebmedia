### Month 2 (June 2026) — SELF-SERVE PROVISIONING
**Theme:** Onboarding goes from "Carlos manually clicks through admin" to "agency owner signs up, gets branded CRM in 30 minutes."

- [ ] Self-serve sub-account creation flow: agency owner picks slug, branding, plan tier; system provisions DB rows + branded login URL + welcome email

- [ ] Custom domain support (CNAME pointing) — tenant's own domain `crm.acmeagency.com` resolves to their branded CRM
- [ ] Stripe Connect integration: tenant connects their Stripe; sub-billing flows through their account
- [ ] Email-from-domain DNS instructions (SPF + DKIM + DMARC) — autogenerated DNS records per tenant
- [ ] First 2 design partners onboarded (free tier, 3-month commitment for feedback)

**Exit criteria:** a new agency owner can self-serve a branded CRM in under 30 minutes without Carlos's intervention.

### Month 3 (July 2026) — AGENCY HIERARCHY

- [ ] Bulk import: agency owner uploads CSV of clients, gets sub-accounts created + invitation emails sent

- [ ] Per-client billing: agency rebills clients at their own markup, NetWebMedia takes platform fee

**Exit criteria:** First paying agency runs 3 client accounts under their brand on NetWebMedia infrastructure.

## Risks

1. **Two CRM backends.** Running `crm-vanilla/` (PHP, normalized) AND `api-php/` (PHP, EAV) AND a Django shelf-ware backend simultaneously is unsustainable. **Pick one before June.**
2. **`compare.html` claims** still mention features (Voice AI, Video Factory, multi-touch attribution) that need real product backing. The build plan above covers most by Month 4-5; track the gap.
3. **GoHighLevel competition.** They're 7 years ahead, $100M+ ARR, 70K+ agency customers. NWM's edge is bilingual EN/ES + AEO-native + Claude-powered. Lean on these — don't compete on feature count.
4. **Solo founder bottleneck.** This roadmap assumes a fractional engineer joins by Month 2. If that doesn't happen, slip everything by 6-8 weeks.
5. **AEO churn.** NetWebMedia's positioning depends on being cited by Claude/ChatGPT/Perplexity. AI search algorithms shift. Maintain blog publishing cadence + YouTube content (per audit) to defend citations.
