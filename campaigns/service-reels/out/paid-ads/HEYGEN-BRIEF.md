# HeyGen Brief — Paid Ads AI Reel

**Use:** HeyGen → "Generate a video from a prompt" (middle-top card on home).
Auto-generates: script + avatar + voiceover + b-roll.

---

## Step 1 — Paste this into the prompt box

```
Create a 30-second vertical (9:16) Instagram Reel for NetWebMedia, an AI-native
marketing agency. This reel promotes our Paid Ads AI service — AI-optimized
campaigns on Google, Meta, TikTok, and LinkedIn with continuous creative testing
and real-time ROAS tracking.

TARGET AUDIENCE: Founders and marketing managers who are spending $3k–$30k/mo
on paid ads but don't know if it's actually working. They've been burned by
agencies that set it and forget it.

TONE: Provocative opener — call out the waste directly. Then solution. Confident
and results-focused. No agency buzz words. No "we're different." Show it.

VISUAL STYLE: Modern, dark-mode tech aesthetic. Deep navy (#010F3B) and black
backgrounds with electric orange (#FF671F) accents. Bold sans-serif type
overlays. B-roll: ad dashboards on MacBooks, analytics charts on iPads,
creative workspaces. High contrast, minimal. Fast cuts to match the energy.

SCRIPT (use exactly this — do not rewrite):

"Stop guessing with your ad budget.
NetWebMedia runs AI-optimized Google, Meta, TikTok, and LinkedIn campaigns —
with real-time ROAS tracking and continuous creative testing.
Our AI finds what converts and doubles down automatically.
Every dollar works harder.
Visit netwebmedia dot com."

SHOT-BY-SHOT DIRECTION:
- 0–3s: Talking head, tight crop, direct eye contact. "Stop guessing with your
  ad budget." — said like a friend calling out bad behavior, not preachy.
- 3–8s: B-roll — overhead table shot with large monitor showing ad campaign
  performance dashboard, team leaning in. Google, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn
  platform logos visible on screen. Energy: urgency.
- 8–14s: B-roll — MacBook + iPad on a clean desk showing real-time analytics
  charts: ROAS graph trending upward, CTR improving week over week.
  Orange trend lines on dark background.
- 14–22s: B-roll — creative workspace flatlay: keyboard, Magic Mouse, iPad
  showing multiple ad creative variants in a testing grid. "What converts"
  highlighted in bright orange on screen.
- 22–26s: Quick title card: "Every dollar works harder." Large bold white
  type on navy, orange underline accent.
- 26–30s: End card — "netwebmedia.com / Starting at $249/mo" bold type on
  dark navy gradient with orange ring accent.

BURNED CAPTIONS: Yes — 2-3 word phrases, large bold white text with black
outline, lower-middle position, synced to VO. Highlight key words (ROAS,
Google Meta TikTok LinkedIn, $249, netwebmedia.com) in electric orange (#FF671F).

END CTA: "netwebmedia.com — Starting at $249/mo"

MUSIC: Driving lo-fi tech beat, 100 BPM (slightly faster than other reels to
match the urgency), mixed at -18 dB under voiceover.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920, MP4, 30fps, H.264, under 30 seconds.
```
