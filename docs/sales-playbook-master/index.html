Which product for which buyer — the decision tree

In a discovery call, these questions route the prospect to the right SKU within 5 minutes.

Q1: Do they want the tool or done-for-you?
Tool = "I'll run my own marketing." Done-for-you = "Run it for me."
Tool → GHL White-Label   or   AEO Audit if they want a written diagnostic first.
Done-for-you → CMO retainer (continue to Q2).
Q2: Do they have multiple decision-makers?
2+ partners or executive sponsors who need to weigh in.
Yes → CMO Premium with synchronous proposal call.
No → CMO Standard with async proposal.
Q3: Are they skeptical / shopping competitors?
Asking lots of "how do you compare to..." or "show me proof of work."
Route through AEO Audit first ($997). The audit IS the proof of work. Auto-credits forward when they upgrade.
Q4: Is their budget < $500/mo total?
Solo founders, very early-stage, or tight constraints.
Either AEO Audit ($997 once) or GHL White-Label ($497 + $197/mo). CMO retainer is not yet a fit — nurture them.
Q5: Are they > $1M revenue + multi-location?
Bigger firms, 10+ team, multiple offices, real ad spend (or zero with regret).
CMO Premium is the natural fit. Synchronous proposal call should be the close moment.
Q6: Are they curious about NWM but not committing?
Reading content, opening emails, not booking calls.
Soft-route to AEO Audit through the Tuesday AEO Brief. $997 buyers ~30% upgrade to retainer within 90 days.

The geometry of the lineup: AEO Audit is the broadest top-of-funnel (cheap, paid, easy). GHL is the parallel tool-only path. CMO Standard is the modal close.
