7. Moving an existing site

Coming from WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or Webflow? We move it over for free with any paid plan. We keep your Google rankings by setting up 301 redirects from your old URLs to the new ones.

What success looks like

  • Within 10 business days: your new site is live on your domain with forms working.
  • Within 30 days: Google starts indexing the new pages. Analytics starts collecting data.
  • Within 90 days: measurable traffic, leads captured through the form, and faster speed than the old site.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Waiting for perfection. Launch at 90%. Tweak the last 10% while it's live.
  • Approving copy without reading it. The copy is what sells. Read every word.
  • Skipping mobile review. More than half your visitors will be on a phone. Check it on your phone before launch.
