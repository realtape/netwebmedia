🚫 Gap #1: No Website

CRITICAL

What's happening

6 of 40 businesses (15%) have zero web presence — no domain, no landing page, nothing. They rely entirely on Instagram profiles, Facebook pages, Fresha listings, or WhatsApp. These include a 24-hour emergency veterinary clinic, three restaurants, a nail salon, and an airport-area hotel visible only through OTA directories.

Why it matters

  • 90% of consumers research a business online before visiting (Google, 2025)
  • 88% of mobile searchers visit or call within 24 hours of a local search
  • Gran Concepcion has 1M+ residents and 100,000+ university students — a digitally native population that defaults to Google for everything
  • AI search engines (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) cannot cite businesses with no indexable web content
  • SOS Vet is a 24-hour emergency veterinary clinic with NO website — pet owners searching "veterinaria urgencia concepcion" at 2 AM will never find them
  • Hotel Terrasur near Carriel Sur Airport has no direct web presence — paying 15-25% OTA commissions on every booking that could be direct

Concepcion context

Concepcion's economy runs on university life, business travel, and regional commerce. Students and young professionals are the most digital-first demographic in Chile — they don't browse Facebook pages or call phone numbers. If your business doesn't appear in Google or AI search, it doesn't exist to 100,000+ students and the professional class that serves them. Emergency vet services without a website are particularly dangerous — pet emergencies happen at night and owners search Google in panic.

🤖 Gap #7: No CRM / Customer Data System

MEDIUM

What's happening

36 of 40 businesses (90%) have no CRM, no customer database, no email list, and no automated follow-up system. Customer relationships exist only in the owner's phone contacts or WhatsApp chat history. Only Barberia Club 34, Bear Barber (AgendaPro), Rekinet (AgendaPro), and Hacienda Patagonia (analytics + pixel tracking) show any form of customer data management.

Why it matters

  • Acquiring a new customer costs 5-7x more than retaining an existing one
  • A 5% increase in retention can boost profits by 25-95% (Harvard Business Review)
  • Without a CRM, there are no birthday emails, no loyalty offers, no re-engagement campaigns
  • Concepcion's student population turns over every 4-5 years — without capturing data, businesses restart from zero every graduating class

  • Dental clinics and spas with no CRM can't send treatment reminders, follow-up care instructions, or reactivation offers
  • If an employee leaves, all their customer relationships leave with them — especially devastating for salons and barbershops
