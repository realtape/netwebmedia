What makes Premium close at higher rates

  1. Self-qualification via $997 audit. Tire-kickers don't pay $997. By the time they're at Stage 2, they're real.
  2. Audit shows competence. The 18-page report demonstrates analytical depth — no further "proof of work" needed.
  3. Audit credit removes objection. The "I already spent $997" sunk-cost works for NWM at the upgrade decision.
  4. Synchronous proposal call kills objections live. Three questions in one call vs. three async email threads over a week.
  5. 4-partner co-sign creates accountability. Once all partners sign, individual buyer's remorse can't unwind the deal.

The Premium playbook in one line.

Sell the audit, deliver the audit, let the audit do the selling, close on a synchronous call with all decision-makers, ship within 7 days.
