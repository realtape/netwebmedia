THE PLAYBOOK
3 LEVERS.
9 WEEKS.
LEVER 01 / KILL DEAD CHANNELS
Cut $40K/mo in zero-signal spend
Used attribution modeling to surface 6 channels with CAC above $6K. Killed all 6. Redirected budget to the 2 channels where CAC was under $800.
LEVER 02 / AI SDR + AEO
Pipeline from zero-cost channels
Deployed AI SDR for outbound. Rebuilt top 10 pages for AEO. Combined: 22 inbound leads/month from ChatGPT citations + 18 meetings from AI SDR. $180/mo total cost.
LEVER 03 / FUNNEL CONVERSION
Triple the close rate
Rebuilt the demo request form as a 3-question qualifier. Leads routed to the right AE pre-briefed. Demo-to-close went from 18% to 41%.
