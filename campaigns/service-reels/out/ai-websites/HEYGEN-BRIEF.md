## Step 1 — Paste this into the prompt box

```
Create a 30-second vertical (9:16) Instagram Reel for NetWebMedia, an AI-native
marketing agency. This reel promotes our AI Websites service — AI-personalized
websites with real-time A/B testing and conversion optimization baked in from
the ground up. Launched in days, not months.

TARGET AUDIENCE: Founders whose websites look fine but don't convert. They
spent $10k–$50k on a web agency and got a brochure. They need a revenue engine
that works while they sleep, not a digital flyer.

TONE: Call out the problem in the first line — the "digital brochure." Then
reframe what a website actually should be: a 24/7 sales machine. Confident,
direct, slightly irreverent toward the old way.

VISUAL STYLE: Modern, dark-mode tech aesthetic. Deep navy (#010F3B) and black
backgrounds with electric orange (#FF671F) accents. Bold sans-serif type
overlays. B-roll: clean minimal desktops and MacBooks, women in modern creative
offices, minimal aesthetic. Shallow depth of field. High contrast. The visuals
should feel like a premium tech product, not a generic agency.

SCRIPT (use exactly this — do not rewrite):

"Your website should be a revenue engine — not a digital brochure.
NetWebMedia builds AI-powered websites that personalize every visitor's experience
in real time, with live A/B testing and conversion optimization built in.
Not just a website. A 24/7 sales machine.
Visit netwebmedia dot com."

SHOT-BY-SHOT DIRECTION:
- 0–5s: Talking head, tight crop, slight smirk. "Not a digital brochure" — 
  said with genuine conviction, not condescension. Modern minimal office
  background.
- 5–12s: B-roll — woman with clean minimal laptop in a modern workspace.
  Screen shows a personalized website experience updating in real time based
  on visitor type. Subtle animation on screen. Calm, premium feeling.
- 12–18s: B-roll — woman working at professional desk, split-screen on monitor
  showing two website variants being A/B tested side by side. Upward-trending
  conversion graph visible. Orange graph line.
- 18–22s: B-roll — MacBook on white minimal desk, shallow depth of field.
  Screen shows a conversion optimization UI with real-time visitor session
  data. Clean, aspirational.
- 22–26s: Title card: "Not just a website. A 24/7 sales machine." Large bold
  white type on navy. Orange underline under "sales machine."
- 26–30s: End card — "netwebmedia.com / Starting at $249/mo" bold type on
  dark navy gradient with orange ring accent.

BURNED CAPTIONS: Yes — 2-3 word phrases, large bold white text with black
outline, lower-middle position, synced to VO. Highlight key words (revenue
engine, A/B testing, 24/7 sales machine, $249, netwebmedia.com) in electric
orange (#FF671F).

END CTA: "netwebmedia.com — Starting at $249/mo"

MUSIC: Subtle lo-fi tech pulse, 90 BPM, mixed at -18 dB under voiceover.
Slightly softer and more premium feeling than the other reels.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920, MP4, 30fps, H.264, under 30 seconds.
```
