# HeyGen Brief — NetWebMedia CMO Package Reel 01

**Use:** HeyGen → "Generate a video from a prompt" (middle-top card on home).
Auto-generates: script + avatar + voiceover + b-roll.

---

## Step 1 — Paste this into the prompt box

```
Create a 30-second vertical (9:16) Instagram Reel for NetWebMedia, an AI-native
marketing agency that replaces traditional CMO retainers for a fraction of the price.

TARGET AUDIENCE: Founders and solo marketers at SMBs who are paying $5,000–$10,000
per month for bloated agency retainers and getting mediocre results.

TONE: Confident, founder-direct, slightly irreverent. Not salesy. No hype words
like "revolutionary" or "game-changing." Plain-English, punchy, short sentences.

VISUAL STYLE: Modern, minimal, dark-mode tech aesthetic. Deep navy and black
backgrounds with electric cyan (#00E0FF) accents. High contrast. Bold sans-serif
type overlays. Cinematic b-roll with shallow depth of field.

SCRIPT (use exactly this — do not rewrite):

"Your agency charges eight thousand a month.
We start at two hundred forty-nine.
Bloated retainers. Six disconnected tools. Zero AEO.
NetWebMedia replaces all of it —
strategy, content, AEO, and a full CRM.
One team. One price. Built for founders who move fast.
Visit netwebmedia dot com. Starting at two hundred forty-nine dollars."

SHOT-BY-SHOT DIRECTION:
- 0–3s: Talking head, tight crop, founder speaks directly to camera.
- 3–8s: B-roll — cluttered desk with multiple screens showing disconnected
  marketing dashboards, sticky notes, dim office lighting. Problem framing.
- 8–12s: Quick cuts — each "disconnected tool" represented by a different
  logo/dashboard flash. Red X marks on screen.
- 12–18s: Clean product UI mockup b-roll — showing a unified dashboard with
  Strategy, Content, AEO, CRM icons in electric cyan.
- 18–24s: Talking head returns, confident close-up, modern minimal office
  with floor-to-ceiling windows in background.
- 24–30s: End card — "netwebmedia.com / Starting at $249" in bold type
  on dark gradient.

BURNED CAPTIONS: Yes — 2-3 word phrases, large bold white text with black
outline, positioned lower-middle, synced to VO. Highlight key words
($249, NetWebMedia, $8,000) in electric cyan.

END CTA: "netwebmedia.com — Starting at $249/mo"

MUSIC: Subtle lo-fi tech pulse, 90 BPM, mixed at -18 dB under voiceover.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920, MP4, 30fps, H.264, under 30 seconds.
```

---

## Step 2 — Avatar selection

**Recommended:** Choose a **Latino male avatar, 30s, business-casual** (charcoal
button-down, no tie). Closest match to your founder persona. HeyGen's "Daniel"
or "Carlos" avatars work well. Avoid overly corporate or stiff avatars.

**Voice:** Male, confident, American English, mid-range. In HeyGen voice
library search for `Guy` or `Tony` (Microsoft neural) — those match our
current typographic-version VO exactly so assets stay consistent.
