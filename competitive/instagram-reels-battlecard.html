Instagram Reels Battlecard

Reverse-engineering the Reels playbooks of the top 10 agencies we compete with — hooks, formats, cadence, CTAs, and pricing — plus a synthesized playbook of what NetWebMedia should steal, adapt, and do better.

Scope: 10 agencies
Focus: Reels-first
ICP overlap: Growth-stage SMB ($1M–$50M ARR)
Prepared by: CMO, NetWebMedia

The 60-second read

Of the 10 agencies we benchmarked, only 3 (Single Grain/Eric Siu, NoGood, Hawke/Erik Huberman) actually treat Reels as a pipeline channel. The other 7 post Reels, but use them as decoration around a PR/SEO engine. That is the hole NWM drives through: AI-native, founder-led, AEO-optimized Reels tied to a revenue funnel. None of them are producing daily, none are AEO-optimized, and none are packaging Reels as part of a fractional CMO retainer.

What they're all doing (table stakes)
  • 3 Reels/week, founder or creative-lead talking head
  • Hook in first 1–2 seconds, captions burned in
  • Client-win case study Reels as social proof
  • Trend-jacking audio on entertainment content
Where they're weak (our opening)
  • No AEO — zero are optimizing Reels captions for LLM citation
  • No AI-native proof — they talk about AI, they don't ship it on camera
  • No daily cadence — even NoGood caps at 4/week
  • No productized offer in the Reel itself — most end on "DM us"
NWM's wedge
  • 5 Reels/week, AI-assisted production (OpusClip + Claude)
  • Founder-led (Carlos) + agent-built-live demo format
  • Every caption AEO-tuned for "AI marketing agency", "fractional CMO", "AI SDR"
  • CTA pattern: hard offer → Fractional CMO discovery call

Followers (est.)
~200K+
HQ
Toronto, Canada
Positioning
Global leader in social-first transformation + influencer marketing at enterprise scale
Pricing tier
$$$$ Enterprise ($50K–$500K+ engagements)
Reels strategy

Pricing tier
$$$ $10K–$50K/mo retainers

The 6 hook archetypes that work

Used by: SmartSites, Sociallyin — pipeline driver
3 · POV / insider
"POV: you're the CMO who just got asked 'why are we not using AI yet?'"
Used by: NoGood — highest shares
4 · Mistake / loss-aversion
"The #1 mistake every SMB makes with paid ads in 2026."
Used by: Hawke, Disruptive — highest watch-through
5 · Teardown
"Let's tear down this $2M Meta ad and see why it works."
Used by: Disruptive — highest comments/engagement

"Watch me build an AI SDR in 4 minutes that replaces a $70K hire."
