Pricing & Carlos26 launch code

What is Carlos26 and how do I use it?
Carlos26 is the NWM CRM launch-week promo code. It applies 50% off for the first 3 months on CMO Starter, Growth, and Scale plans (as well as standalone CRM plans). Enter it at checkout or click any "Start with 50% off" button on this site — the code will be pre-filled. Valid for 7 days or the first 500 activations, whichever comes first.
Which plans does Carlos26 apply to?
Carlos26 is valid on: CMO Starter ($249→$124/mo), CMO Growth ($999→$499/mo), CMO Scale ($1,999→$999/mo), and on the standalone CRM Starter / Pro / Agency SKUs. It does not stack with other discounts or with annual pre-pay.
What happens after the 3-month discount period?
Your plan automatically resumes the standard monthly rate starting month 4. We email you 15 days before the discount ends so there are no surprises. You can downgrade or cancel at any time from the billing page in your CRM.
How do I pay — what payment processors do you support?
We bill through Mercado Pago (credit card, debit card, bank transfer in Chile; major cards internationally). Monthly auto-renewal is handled via MP's preapproval flow. Setup fees are charged once at signup. USD pricing is displayed for clarity; actual charges are in CLP at the current FX rate.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime from the billing page — we'll keep your account active until the end of the current billing cycle, then archive your data for 90 days in case you come back.

NWM CRM

What makes NWM CRM different from HubSpot / Salesforce / GoHighLevel?
NWM CRM is purpose-built for SMBs running bilingual (EN/ES) campaigns. Three concrete differences: (1) AI agents are native — SDR, Booking, Voice — not upsells; (2) no per-seat pricing — one flat monthly fee; (3) wired to NWM CMS out of the box, so every form, page, and campaign link lives in one pipeline. We're not trying to replace Salesforce for a 200-rep team. We're replacing the Zapier-glued stack most SMBs run.
How many contacts can I import?
Starter: up to 2,500 contacts. Growth: up to 25,000. Scale: unlimited. CSV and JSON imports are supported, plus direct migration from HubSpot, GoHighLevel, and Pipedrive via one-click connectors. Duplicates are auto-merged based on email.
Does NWM CRM send email campaigns? Is deliverability good?
Yes — template, drip sequence, or one-off sends. We ship with SPF / DKIM / DMARC preconfigured for the netwebmedia.com domain and optional custom-domain setup on Growth+. Our internal send infrastructure auto-excludes unsubscribed emails and bounced addresses before every send. Typical open rates on our own 2,400-contact database: 28–47%.
Can I white-label NWM CRM for my clients?
White-label is included on CMO Scale and the standalone CRM Agency plan. You get your own subdomain, logo, and login experience, plus unlimited client sub-accounts billed at your own rate. Full revenue share model — no NWM branding required.
What AI agents come with the CRM?
Starter includes the basic campaign editor. Growth adds: AI SDR (writes personalized follow-ups), AI Booking Agent (handles inbound calls + schedules 24/7), and AI Voice (phone answering). Scale adds custom agents trained on your own knowledge base. All agents are Claude-powered under the hood.

NWM CMS

Is NWM CMS a replacement for WordPress?
For most SMBs — yes. NWM CMS generates pages, blog posts, and landing pages with AI, ships bilingual content by default, and has SEO + AEO optimization built in. No plugins, no theme hell, no maintenance windows. If you need complex plugin ecosystems (WooCommerce, LearnDash, etc.), WordPress may still be the right choice for now.
Can I use my own domain and design?
Yes. Point your domain (DNS A / CNAME record) at our servers and we'll provision SSL automatically. Custom design is available on Growth+ — send us your brand guidelines or an existing Figma file and we'll tune the AI agents to match.
How does the AI blog writer decide what to write?
You give the CMS your niche, target keywords, and competitors. The SEO Planner does keyword research, clusters them by topical authority, and drafts 4–16 articles per month depending on plan. You review drafts in the editor before they go live. Every article ships with FAQ JSON-LD, meta description, and AEO-optimized hooks for Claude / Perplexity / Gemini.
Can NWM CMS work without NWM CRM?
Yes — they're separately purchasable. Forms submit to any endpoint of your choice, including HubSpot, Mailchimp, or a Zapier webhook. That said, 70% of customers bundle both because the form→contact→campaign→dashboard loop is seamless when they're joined.

Onboarding & migration

How long does onboarding take?
Starter: self-serve, live in < 1 hour. Growth: guided 3-day onboarding with a success manager (import, pipeline setup, first campaign). Scale: 2-week white-glove migration + team training.
Can you migrate my data from my current CRM?
Yes. We have one-click importers for HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Pipedrive, and Salesforce. CSV is supported for everything else. We map your existing stages, custom fields, and tags during migration so nothing is lost. Migration is free on Growth and Scale; Starter adds a $149 one-time migration fee if you want us to handle it.
What if I'm not technical — can I really self-serve?
Yes. Starter is designed for non-technical founders. The setup wizard walks you through: connect your email domain, import contacts (drag-drop CSV), pick a pipeline template for your niche, and send your first campaign. If you get stuck, chat support is in every plan and average response is under 2 hours.

Security, privacy & unsubscribe

Where is my data stored? Is it GDPR-compliant?
Data is stored on InMotion Hosting servers (SOC 2 Type II) in the United States, with encrypted backups every 6 hours. We are GDPR and LGPD-aligned: you can export or delete all your data at any time from the settings page. Unsubscribe requests are honored globally — any contact on your unsubscribe list is automatically excluded from all future sends across all your campaigns.
Who sees my pipeline and contacts?
Only you and the team seats you invite. Role-based access: superadmin (NWM internal support, read-only, only with your explicit permission via support ticket), admin (your main account, full control), and user (restricted to assigned pipelines). Audit logs are retained for 12 months.
How does the unsubscribe system work?
Every outgoing email includes a one-click unsubscribe link in the footer (CAN-SPAM and GDPR compliant). When a contact clicks it, their email is added to a global unsubscribes table tied to your account — they will never receive another campaign from you, even if their email appears in a future list you import. You can view and manage the list at /crm/marketing.html#unsubscribes.
