3 · The top-3 reasons you're missing

Reason 1 · No FAQPage schema on practice pages. ChatGPT cites firms with structured FAQ markup 3.2× more than firms without. Your 5 practice pages have zero schema today. This is the single biggest leverage point.
Reason 2 · Anchor pages are 280–420 words. Citation-worthy practice content runs 1,800–2,200 words with question-answer structure. Your pages are short brochure copy. Answer engines pass them over for longer, deeper alternatives.
Reason 3 · No location specificity. Your pages name "Jamie Test PLLC" but never name the city you serve. Engines like Perplexity weight geo-explicit content for location queries. This is a 30-minute fix per page.

  • Weeks 7–10: Add location-specific landing pages for your top 3 cities × 2 anchor practice areas (6 pages).
