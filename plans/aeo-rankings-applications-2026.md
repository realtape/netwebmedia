## Post-listing action plan

If you get listed (which is likely with the survey + methodology):

1. **Update website:**
   - Add badges/trust signals to services.html footer
   - Link back to ranking page (good for SEO)
   - Update about.html with award mentions

2. **Social amplification:**
   - LinkedIn: "Proud to be listed in [Ranking]. Here's how we're redefining AEO for SMBs..."
   - Twitter: Quote the ranking mention + link
   - Email: Send to customer list ("We're recognized as a Top AEO Agency")

3. **Sales enablement:**
   - Create one-pager: "NetWebMedia: Award-Recognized AEO Agency"
   - Add to sales deck: recognition section
   - Use in outbound emails: "As seen in [Ranking]" social proof

4. **Compound effect:**
   - Once you're in 1 ranking, it's easier to get into the next
   - Rankings cite each other ("Also listed in X, Y, Z")
   - Creates a citation loop that persists for months
