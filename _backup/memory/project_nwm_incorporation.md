NetWebMedia is incorporated in Chile (not in a US state) but its target market is US small and medium businesses.

**Why:** This affects governing law choices in contracts (Chilean law, not Delaware), and should not trigger assumptions about US incorporation.

**How to apply:** When drafting or reviewing legal language — governing law, jurisdiction, arbitration rules — use "Laws of the Republic of Chile" and ICDR (International Centre for Dispute Resolution, the international arm of the AAA) rather than a US state + domestic AAA.
