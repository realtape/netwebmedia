10Measurement Framework

Attribution model: Multi-touch, position-based (40% first touch / 40% last touch / 20% middle). Tracked via UTM + self-reported source on intake form + server-side GA4.

Dashboards to build in Looker Studio (4 boards)

BoardAudienceRefreshKey widgets
Pipeline & RevenueCarlos dailyHourlyMRR, new MRR, churned MRR, pipeline coverage ratio, % to plan
Channel performanceCMO weeklyDailyCAC by channel, LTV:CAC, payback, MQL→SQL→Won funnel
Content / AEOcontent-strategist weeklyDailyLLM citations (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity), organic visits, MQLs per post
OperationsCarlos weeklyDailyNPS, CSAT, time-to-first-value, onboarding completion %, churn risk score

Review cadence

  • Daily (5 min): Carlos checks Pipeline & Revenue board
  • Weekly Monday (30 min): CMO + Carlos walk all 4 boards; decide one cut, one double-down
  • Monthly (60 min): Full retro vs forecast; update scenario position (bear/base/bull)
  • Quarterly (half day): Strategic review; reallocate channel budgets; refresh competitive battlecards

12Brand Guardrails (marketing-facing)

  • No public discounts below list. The “Carlos26” promo is retired by July 1.
  • No sub-$500k revenue clients. Below the brand.
  • Partners whitelabel the software, not the NetWebMedia agency brand.
  • All marketing follows the Gulf-Oil palette (Navy #010F3B / Orange #FF671F / Blue #4A90D9) — see Brand Book.
  • Bilingual by default for LATAM — not translation, localization (voice/tone shifts).
  • Every claim (“4.2× citations”, “40% cost cut”) ties to a published case study or data study. No manufactured proof.
