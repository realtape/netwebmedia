## 7. Risk & Contingency

### Risk: Instagram algorithm suppression on AEO carousels
**Mitigation:** Diversify hashtag stacks weekly (don't repeat the exact 12 tags); rotate carousel families A/B/C to signal variety to the platform's content classifier. Mirror critical posts to Facebook so reach loss isn't fatal.

### Risk: Low engagement if CTA isn't clear
**Mitigation:** The `/aeo-index.html` link is the single point-of-action. Test whether embedding the URL in the bio link (rotate carousel week → story click) or in story stickers improves CTR. A/B by week.

### Risk: Carousel completion rate (users swipe all 5 slides)
**Mitigation:** Slide 5 has the strongest CTA. The slide counter (1/5...5/5) signals progress and encourages completion. Monitor Instagram Insights → Carousel swipe-through rate. If <25%, retool headline or reduce slide count to 3 in Week 2.
