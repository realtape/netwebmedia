From the kitchen

"My grandmother ran a small trattoria outside Genova. She insisted every plate taste like the place it came from. At Maré, I cook the way she taught me — restraint, patience, and the best ingredient doing the least amount of work."

Chef Giovanni Caruso trained at Uliassi, Osteria Francescana, and Quince. Maré is his first solo restaurant. Three Michelin Bib Gourmands. James Beard Rising Chef finalist 2024.

— Giovanni Caruso, Chef / Owner
