Frequently asked

What is a Fractional CMO?

A senior marketing leader who runs your marketing strategy part-time, for a fraction of a full-time CMO's cost. Our version adds a full AI-powered execution team — strategy and delivery bundled, not just advice.

Why is this cheaper than a solo Fractional CMO?

A solo operator charges for personal hours. We productized the role: senior strategy plus an AI execution stack and a standing team with near-zero marginal cost — leadership and execution without solo rates.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. During the Founding Client Sprint, Growth and Premium include a guarantee: qualified leads in 30 days or your first month is refunded.

Which tier should I start with?

Under ~$3M or testing the relationship: AEO Starter. $3–10M without a marketing team: CMO Growth (the flagship). $10M+ or PE-backed: CMO Premium. You can move up the ladder anytime.
