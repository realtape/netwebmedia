FAQ

The questions every Growth client asks on the fit call.

What does CMO Growth actually deliver each month?

A full fractional CMO function: monthly AEO audit + execution, one niche landing page built and managed, 8 AI-assisted content pieces (Reels + blog), CRM management with email automation, paid ads management across Google + Meta (ad spend at cost + 12% mgmt fee, $300/mo min), AI SDR with lead qualification, and an async monthly strategy report from a human strategist.

How does Growth compare to hiring a fractional CMO?

US fractional CMOs on Toptal, MarketerHire and Chief Outsiders bill $5,000–$15,000/mo for strategy only — they don't execute. CMO Growth is $999/mo and includes both strategy and execution: content, ads, CRM, SDR. You're not replacing a CMO; you're replacing the entire marketing team they would have managed.

What's the Founding Client guarantee?

We commit to a minimum number of qualified leads in your first 30 days based on the scope we agree to. If we miss it, your first month is fully refunded — no fine print, no fight. The threshold is set per industry during onboarding so it's fair to both sides.

What does the $499 setup fee cover?

CRM provisioning and tenant setup, AEO baseline audit, brand voice intake, paid ads account audit and access, conversion tracking install, niche landing page wireframe, and pipeline configuration. It's waived on annual prepay.

How does the paid ads management fee work?

You pay ad platforms (Google, Meta) directly at cost using your own card. We charge a 12% management fee on the ad spend, with a $300/mo minimum. So if you spend $2,500 on ads, the management fee is $300; if you spend $5,000, it's $600. No markups, no kickbacks, no hidden margin.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes — after the 90-day minimum, cancel any time by emailing hello@netwebmedia.com. No cancellation fees, no penalties. You keep every asset we built — landing page, content, CRM data, all of it.

How is Growth different from Premium?

Growth ($999/mo) covers AEO, SEO, paid ads on Google + Meta, 8 content pieces, AI SDR, and CRM. Premium ($2,490/mo) adds 16 short-form videos + 2 YouTube long-form per month, TikTok ads, a dedicated account strategist, custom AI agents, voice AI, and white-glove onboarding.

What industries does Growth work best for?

The 14 niches NetWebMedia is built for: hospitality, restaurants, healthcare, beauty, SMB, legal, real estate, local specialist, automotive, education, events & weddings, financial services, home services, wine & agriculture. We have niche-specific playbooks for each.
