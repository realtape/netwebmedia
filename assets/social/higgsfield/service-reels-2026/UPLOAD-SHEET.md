### Posting tips
- **Instagram:** easiest from the phone app → Reels → pick the MP4 → paste Content → (hashtags in caption or first comment). Set location: Santiago, Chile.
- **Facebook Page:** Page → Create reel → upload the MP4 → paste the same caption.
- Confirm the **bio link** points where you want before posting (captions end in "link in bio").
- Spacing 1–2 per day beats posting all 9 at once (protects reach).
