Our investment philosophy

Four principles that have governed how we invest every client dollar since 1998.

01

Costs compound against you

Every 1% in annual fees is 28% less wealth over 30 years. We target all-in expense ratios under 0.18%.

02

Markets work — don't fight them

Active managers underperform their index ~85% of the time over 15 years. We build around capital markets, not around predictions.

03

Diversify across what actually matters

Factor tilts toward value, size, profitability and duration — not 50 ETFs that all own the same seven names.

04

Behavior is the last alpha source

The biggest wealth destroyer isn't the market — it's selling low in a panic. Our job is to keep you in your seat.

Why fiduciary matters
