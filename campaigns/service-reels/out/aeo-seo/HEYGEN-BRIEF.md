## Step 1 — Paste this into the prompt box

```

VISUAL STYLE: Modern, dark-mode tech aesthetic. Deep navy (#010F3B) and black
backgrounds with electric orange (#FF671F) accents. Bold sans-serif type
overlays. B-roll: laptop screens showing search results, AI chat interfaces,
analytics dashboards. High contrast, minimal.

SCRIPT (use exactly this — do not rewrite):

"When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity for the best marketing solution —
will they find you.

ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity —

SHOT-BY-SHOT DIRECTION:

- 6–12s: B-roll — woman at desktop monitor typing a query into ChatGPT

- 12–22s: B-roll — split montage of four AI platform logos (ChatGPT, Gemini,
  Claude, Perplexity) each flipping to show a brand mention in the answer.

- 22–26s: B-roll — iMac showing a content analytics dashboard with 40+ green
  "published" indicators and rising traffic graph in orange.
- 26–30s: End card — "netwebmedia.com / Starting at $249/mo" bold type on
  dark navy gradient with orange ring accent.

BURNED CAPTIONS: Yes — 2-3 word phrases, large bold white text with black
outline, lower-middle position, synced to VO. Highlight key words (ChatGPT,
Perplexity, AEO, $249, netwebmedia.com) in electric orange (#FF671F).

END CTA: "netwebmedia.com — Starting at $249/mo"

MUSIC: Subtle lo-fi tech pulse, 90 BPM, mixed at -18 dB under voiceover.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920, MP4, 30fps, H.264, under 30 seconds.
```
