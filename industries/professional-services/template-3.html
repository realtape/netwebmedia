How we work

Six-step methodology refined over 180+ engagements. Every engagement ends with something shipped — not a slide deck.

1

Immersion

Two weeks on-site (or Zoom) interviewing your team, mapping systems, and reading the last 6 months of metrics. We come in with questions, not answers.

2

Diagnosis

Written findings + a prioritized opportunity map. We rank every lever by impact × feasibility so you can say no to 80% of them without guilt.

3

Strategy

One-page strategy. Not a deck. A single page your CEO can print and hang on the wall.

4

Build

We embed with your team for the actual execution — process redesign, tooling rollout, hiring sprints, M&A diligence — whatever the strategy requires.

5

Transfer

Documentation, training, and a 90-day handoff period. We don't create dependency. We create operators.

6

Check-In

Quarterly pulse checks for 12 months post-engagement. Included. No hourly billing.
