Creating the Free CRM account

The "Free CRM" CTA on pricing routes to /register.html?plan=free. This is the entry point we want every prospect to cross.

  1. Full name — typed as Carlos Test
  2. Work email — carlos@chilespirits.com
  3. Password — min 8 characters
  4. Terms checkbox — required (GDPR / CCPA / Chile Ley 19.628 compliance)
  5. Create account — server-side: creates an organization, seeds 14-niche allowlist, generates auth token, redirects to /app/
Why "Free CRM" first? Friction is the enemy. No credit card, under 2 minutes, immediate access to a working pipeline tool. Once a prospect's contacts and deals live in NWM, the Fractional CMO upsell sells itself.

First-run: pick your industry

The very first time a client opens /crm-vanilla/, NWM asks one question. The answer reshapes pipeline stages, KPI definitions, and email templates for that niche — all 14 are first-class.

Critical: Niche selection is a one-time decision that reshapes the entire CRM. Pipeline stages, KPI templates, and email automation are all niche-aware. Clients can change it later in Settings but it's worth taking 60 seconds here.
