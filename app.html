Frequently asked

When will the app launch?

TestFlight and Android closed beta start shortly. Public App Store + Google Play listings drop once Apple and Google complete review (typically 1–2 weeks after submission).

Is the app free?

Yes. The app is free. You need an active NetWebMedia account (any plan, starting $249/mo) to sign in.

Does it work without the website?

The app talks to the same netwebmedia.com/api endpoints the website uses. Your data stays in sync either way.

What about my existing login?

Use the same email and password as the web dashboard. Biometric unlock (Face ID / fingerprint) ships in v0.2.

Does it support Spanish?

Yes. The app follows your NetWebMedia account language preference. English and Spanish at launch.
