🚫 Gap #1: No Website

CRITICAL

What's happening

5 of 40 businesses (12.5%) have zero web presence — no domain, no landing page, nothing. They rely entirely on Facebook pages, WhatsApp, or OTA listings. Two more have websites that are down or non-functional.

Why it matters

  • 90% of consumers research a business online before visiting (Google, 2025)
  • 88% of mobile searchers visit or call within 24 hours of a local search
  • Without a website, there is no owned channel — the business is entirely at the mercy of third-party platforms
  • AI search engines (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) cannot cite businesses that have no indexable web content
  • In Santiago's premium neighborhoods (Providencia, Vitacura, Las Condes), having no website signals "unprofessional" to high-value customers

Santiago context

Santiago is Chile's economic powerhouse with 7.1 million metro residents and a massive tourist/business travel market. Neighborhoods like Bellavista, Providencia, and Las Condes have extremely high search volumes for local services. Businesses with no website are invisible to all of them.

MEDIUM

What's happening

36 of 40 businesses (90%) have no CRM, no customer database, no email list, and no automated follow-up system. Customer relationships exist only in the owner's phone contacts or WhatsApp chat history.

Why it matters

  • Acquiring a new customer costs 5-7x more than retaining an existing one
  • A 5% increase in retention can boost profits by 25-95% (Harvard Business Review)
  • Without a CRM, there are no birthday emails, no loyalty offers, no re-engagement campaigns

  • Hotels/hostals that don't capture guest emails can never market return visits or referrals
