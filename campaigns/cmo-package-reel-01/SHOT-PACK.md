# CMO Package — IG Reel 01 (Shot Pack)

**Target:** 30s vertical 9:16 Instagram Reel
**Campaign:** NetWebMedia CMO Package launch — "$500/mo replaces your agency"
**Persona:** SMB founder / solo marketer drowning in tools & retainers
**Tone:** Founder-direct, confident, punchy

---

## Voiceover Script (≈28s at 1.05x, ~60 words)

> Your agency charges eight thousand a month. We do it for five hundred.
> Bloated retainers. Six disconnected tools. Zero AEO.
> NetWebMedia replaces all of it — strategy, content, AEO, and a full CRM.
> One team. One price. Built for founders who move fast.
> Visit netwebmedia dot com. First month — five hundred dollars.

**Recommended VO tool:** ElevenLabs (v3, "Brian" or "Adam") — male, confident, mid-30s, American.
**Pacing:** 1.0–1.05x. Leave a 300ms beat after each period.

## Brand Spec

- **Primary BG:** `#0A0E27` (deep navy) → `#000` gradient
- **Accent:** `#00E0FF` (electric cyan) — use for CTAs, numbers, icons
- **Text:** `#FFFFFF` white
- **Font:** Inter or SF Pro, Bold for headlines
- **Logo:** N monogram, electric cyan
- **Safe area:** Keep text 10% inside edges — IG UI crops top/bottom
