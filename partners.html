Partner program FAQ

Can I white-label everything under my domain?

Yes. CRM, CMS, Email, Video Factory, and AI CMO all support full white-labeling: your domain (e.g., app.youragency.com), your logo, your email sender addresses, your color palette. Your clients never see the NetWebMedia brand.

What's the minimum commitment?

90-day minimum on any white-label tier, then month-to-month with 30 days written notice. This gives you runway to land your first clients before making long-term decisions. Annual prepay gets you 10% off.

Who handles the client billing?

You do. You bill your clients directly on your invoice, at your price. We invoice you monthly for the white-label platform. Your clients are your clients — we have no contact with them unless you explicitly introduce us for escalations.

What support do I get as a partner?

Private Slack channel with our product + engineering teams. Partner onboarding (45-min call), co-branded sales decks, pitch scripts, and ROI calculators for your prospects. Full-Stack partners get a dedicated partner success manager and quarterly strategy calls.

Can I customize features or integrate custom code?

Yes. Full API access is included on all partner tiers. Custom workflows via Zapier, Make, or n8n. If you need a deeper customization, our team quotes it separately — typical partner customizations are 1–3 week scopes.

What if I want to offer services you don't currently white-label?

Talk to us. We're building the partner catalog based on demand. Current roadmap includes white-label paid ads reporting, white-label analytics dashboards, and white-label AI chatbots — expected Q2–Q3 2026.

How do I provision new client sub-accounts?

Through your partner dashboard in seconds: enter client name, domain, admin email → new sub-account spins up, branded under your domain. You can pre-configure defaults (pipelines, email templates, brand voice) so every new client starts ready to sell.

Do you offer revenue share or profit share models?

Flat monthly platform cost is the default — you keep 100% of the margin above it. For high-volume partners (50+ clients) we offer custom revenue-share structures with lower platform costs in exchange for minimum commitments. Contact us to discuss.

What's the fastest I can be selling?

3–7 days from signed partner agreement to live white-label instance. We've had partners close their first client within 14 days of onboarding. The Full-Stack tier ships with a sales-ready demo environment pre-loaded with sample data.

Is there a revenue minimum?

No minimum client count or revenue commitment. You pay only your white-label platform fee (starting $249/mo) whether you have 1 client or 100. We grow when you grow.
