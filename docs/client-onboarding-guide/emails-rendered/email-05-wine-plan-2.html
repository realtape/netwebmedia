3 growth levers for Chile Spirits, Carlos

Here's what the analysis turned up. Three strategies that help wineries and agricultural producers grow their brand and revenue beyond the cellar door:

1. Build a DTC e-commerce channel with an automated wine club acquisition funnel. Selling direct-to-consumer through your own channel — not through marketplaces or distributors — is where the margin is. We build a DTC e-commerce setup with a wine club enrollment funnel, automated club member nurture (tasting notes, seasonal offers, harvest updates), and a retention sequence that reduces churn and increases lifetime member value.

2. Tell your wine story with AI-powered content that reaches international buyers and enthusiasts. The modern wine buyer discovers new wines through content — blog posts, tasting notes, vintage stories, and winemaker profiles — not just in-store displays. We create a content strategy that tells the story of your terroir, your winemaking philosophy, and your vintages in a way that attracts buyers searching online and positions your brand in export markets you want to enter.

3. Build a digital hospitality funnel that converts cellar door visitors into long-term club members. The cellar door visit is your best conversion opportunity — but most wineries don't have a system to capture visitor information and convert them into club members after they leave. We build a post-visit automation sequence (email + WhatsApp) that keeps the experience alive, drives online purchases, and converts first-time visitors into recurring revenue.
