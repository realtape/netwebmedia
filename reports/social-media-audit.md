**Date:** April 28, 2026

**Subject:** Full digital presence review for NetWebMedia (netwebmedia.com)

---

## Executive Summary

NetWebMedia was founded in July 2025 and is approximately 9 months old at audit date. The brand has staked out social media handles consistently across all major platforms (@netwebmedia on Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok), which is a strong foundation. However, because the agency is so young and social platforms block automated scraping, follower counts and engagement metrics cannot be independently verified without manual login. The critical gap is not follower count — it is the absence of any third-party validation: no Clutch profile, no Google Business Profile reviews indexed, no press mentions, and no appearance in any fractional CMO agency ranking list searched. The social media infrastructure exists; the authority signals do not yet.

## Priority Gaps — Ranked by Business Impact

### GAP 1: No Third-Party Credibility (Critical)
Zero Clutch, G2, or Trustpilot presence means no social proof for buyers doing due diligence. A buyer landing on netwebmedia.com who then searches for "NetWebMedia reviews" will find nothing — and nothing reads as "too new to trust."

**Action:** Create a Clutch.co profile immediately (free). Submit 3 client reviews (even from beta/pilot clients). This alone can appear in Google search results within 30 days.

### GAP 2: Google Business Profile Not Verified (Critical)
The GMB link on the website redirects to a generic Google search rather than a verified panel. This means the Google Reviews link in the footer is functionally broken for trust-building purposes.

**Action:** Complete GMB verification this week. Add category, description, service area. Immediately request 3-5 reviews from any existing contacts.

### GAP 3: X / Twitter Handle Not Confirmed (High)
The website does not link to X, and no @netwebmedia handle was found in searches. Given that X is the primary platform for AI and marketing thought leadership, this is a missed distribution channel.

**Action:** Claim @netwebmedia on X immediately if available. Begin posting 1-2x daily with AI marketing commentary, campaign results, and fractional CMO positioning.

### GAP 4: YouTube Channel Has No Indexed Content (High)
YouTube is the highest-leverage long-form content platform for an AI-native agency. No content found in any search. This also undermines AEO credibility — an agency offering AEO services should itself be present in AI-indexed video content.

**Action:** Publish first 3 videos within 30 days. Prioritize: (1) "What is an AI fractional CMO?" (2) "How NetWebMedia works" (3) "AI marketing audit — what we found in 48 hours."

### GAP 5: No Press or Backlink Coverage (High)
Not a single press mention or external site backlinking to netwebmedia.com was found in search. This suppresses domain authority and organic rankings for the service keywords NWM targets.

**Action:** Submit to HARO/Connectively for marketing journalist queries. Write a guest post for one marketing publication (MarketingProfs, Search Engine Journal, or similar). Get listed on at least 2 AI tools directories (there.is/tools, FutureTools, etc.).

### GAP 6: Instagram Profile Name Confusion (Medium)
The Instagram account is indexed as "Carlos Martinez (@netwebmedia)" — the brand name does not appear in the display name. This splits search visibility between the founder's personal brand and the agency brand.

**Action:** Update Instagram display name to "NetWebMedia | AI Fractional CMO" and ensure bio includes the $249/mo price, a link to netwebmedia.com, and a clear call to action.

### GAP 7: LinkedIn Company Page Not Claimed (Low — per Carlos's strategy)
No LinkedIn company page exists at linkedin.com/company/netwebmedia. Per Carlos's decision, LinkedIn is not used for distribution. However, the page should be claimed as a placeholder to prevent squatting and provide directory presence.

**Action (minimum):** Claim the page, add website URL and one-line description. No posting required.
