## 1. Executive Summary

Today we shipped three conversion fixes that close the loop between AEO traffic and revenue: three anonymized client-win cards on the homepage (replacing a buried single case study), a visible 6-Q FAQ accordion on `/pricing.html` mirroring the FAQPage JSON-LD, and an above-the-fold newsletter capture on `/blog.html`. The 24-post AEO content cluster — two pillar posts across all 14 niches — went live the same day. The site is now built to convert the traffic AEO is sending us, not just attract it.

**The bet.** AEO citation in ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews compounds faster than paid social, and our content advantage (24 niche-targeted pillars + the `/aeo-index.html` scoring tool) is a moat competitors can't catch in 90 days. We will treat email (Tuesday AEO Brief) as our X/LinkedIn replacement, Instagram + YouTube as discovery, and paid Meta + Google PMax as accelerant — not as the engine. Every dollar must trace to either an AEO Audit ($997, 100% credited toward retainer) or a CMO Growth retainer ($999/mo).

**The guard-rails.** No vanity metrics — impressions without a pipeline tie are deleted from the dashboard. No LinkedIn, no X, ever, per Carlos's standing decision. WhatsApp Business broadcasts hold until WABA verification clears (target June). TikTok stays parked until Q3. If paid CPMs blow past $25 sustained, we cut and reallocate to organic content velocity. Brand debt compounds faster than tech debt — anything below the navy/orange brand bar gets killed before it ships.

## 5. Paid Ads Plan — Meta + Google PMax

**No paid LinkedIn, no paid X.** Paid is acceleration, not engine. Three budget scenarios:

### $1,500/mo tier — "Validate"
- **Meta (FB+IG): $900** — 1 audience, 3 creatives, route to `/aeo-index.html`.
- **Google PMax: $600** — branded + AEO long-tail. Route to `/pricing.html`.
- **Goal:** 12 leads/mo, 2 audits, 0–1 retainer. Target CAC ≤ $750.

### $3,000/mo tier — "Scale validated wins"
- **Meta: $1,800** — 2 audiences (law firm owners + multi-location health), 3 creatives each. Routes split: 60% `/aeo-index.html`, 40% niche industry hub (e.g. `/industries/legal-services/`).
- **Google PMax: $1,200** — full-funnel asset group; conversion goal = audit purchase.
- **Goal:** 25 leads/mo, 5 audits, 2 retainers. Target CAC ≤ $600.

### $6,000/mo tier — "Compound"
- **Meta: $3,500** — 4 audiences (law, health, real estate, financial), retargeting layer added. Lookalike from newsletter list (once ≥ 500 subs).
- **Google PMax: $2,500** — multi-asset-group; one group per top-4 niche; demand-gen layer added.
- **Goal:** 50 leads/mo, 12 audits, 4 retainers. Target CAC ≤ $500.

### Creative directions (3 per channel, all channels)

**Meta (FB+IG):**
1. **"Are you cited by ChatGPT?"** — 6-second hook → score teaser → `/aeo-index.html`. Carousel + Reel variants from `assets/social/carousels/a-slide-{1..5}.svg`.
2. **"Anonymous client win"** — pull from the 3 homepage cards. Story: problem → AEO audit → 60-day result. Routes to `/pricing.html`.
3. **"$997 → 100% credit"** — direct-response. Audit price + retainer credit anchor. Routes to checkout.

**Google PMax:**
1. **Branded + niche-modifier** — "AEO agency for [niche]" exact + phrase. Lands on industry hub.
2. **Long-tail AEO queries** — "how to get cited by ChatGPT," "AEO vs SEO," "ChatGPT optimization agency." Lands on top pillar of relevant niche.
3. **Audit-intent terms** — "marketing audit small business," "AEO audit." Lands on `/pricing.html` (audit anchor).

### Audience strategy
- **Cold:** Interest stacks: "small business owner" + "marketing software" + 35–60. Geo: US + LATAM Spanish-speaking.
- **Warm:** Site visitors 30 days, video-viewed 50%+, IG-engaged 90 days.
- **Hot:** Newsletter list lookalike (1%) — only fires once list ≥ 500.
