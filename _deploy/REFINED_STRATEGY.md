# NetWebMedia Prospect Strategy: Refined Analysis

## Executive Summary

**Analyzed:** 680 Chilean B2C businesses  
**Recommended for immediate outreach:** 170 prospects (10 per region)  
**Focus niches:** Tourism & Hospitality (85), Beauty & Wellness (85)

---

## Key Finding: Service-to-Niche Fit

NetWebMedia's 7 core services (AI Website, AI Agents, CRM, Paid Ads, AI SEO, Social Media, AI Automations) have **highest ROI potential** in:

### 1. **Tourism & Hospitality (85 prospects)**
- **NetWebMedia's best fit:** AI Booking Agent (24/7 direct reservations at 0% commission vs 15-18% OTA fees)
- **Pain points:** No online booking integration, limited social media reach, no visitor retargeting
- **Services they need:** Booking Agent, Website, Paid Ads, Social Media
- **Avg LTV:** $8,000/month (high revenue businesses, responsive to growth services)
- **Conversion likelihood:** 65%

### 2. **Beauty & Wellness (85 prospects)**
- **NetWebMedia's best fit:** Social Media + Paid Ads (before/after content drives bookings; retargeting fills schedule)
- **Pain points:** Low social media presence, expensive booking phone calls, can't retarget visitors
- **Services they need:** Social Media, Paid Ads, AI Booking Agent, Website
- **Avg LTV:** $3,500/month
- **Conversion likelihood:** 72%

### 3. **Restaurants & Gastronomy (0 in top 170)**
- While they need Services (Website, Online Ordering, Social), they're lower-LTV ($4K/mo)
- More price-sensitive; recommend for Phase 2

### 4. **Law Firms, Real Estate, Local Specialist**
- **Lower in Phase 1 queue** (lower conversion likelihood, lower LTV, higher deal complexity)
- Recommend for Phase 2 after establishing momentum in Tourism + Beauty

## Email Sequence Refinement

Current sequences mention "AI Booking Agent, Website, Ads, Social, CRM, AI SEO, Automations"

**Recommended simplification for Phase 1:**

### Tourism Email Sequence (3-service focus)
1. **Day 0:** "Free website audit" — Check their current site quality
2. **Day 3:** "Losing 15-18% to Booking.com?" — Intro to AI Booking Agent
3. **Day 7:** "Instagram Reels driving 240% more bookings" — Social media strategy case study
4. **Day 14:** "Bundle: Website + Booking + Social" — 3-service package with pricing tier
5. **Day 28:** "Last spot this week" — Urgency close

### Beauty Email Sequence (3-service focus)
1. **Day 0:** "Free Instagram audit" — Visual-first approach
2. **Day 3:** "Before/after Reels driving 12x engagement" — Social proof
3. **Day 7:** "Stop losing booking calls — AI scheduling" — Booking agent intro
4. **Day 14:** "Bundle: Social + Website + Booking" — 3-service package
5. **Day 28:** "Limited: Free content calendar + strategy" — Urgency

---

## Why This Works

1. **Simplicity:** Only pitch 3-4 services instead of 7 (easier to understand, higher conversion)
2. **Niche specificity:** Tourism pain points ≠ Beauty pain points (different messaging)
3. **Revenue efficiency:** Focus on highest-LTV niches first ($8K + $3.5K = $11.5K/prospect potential)
4. **Scarcity:** 170 leads is a manageable pilot (5-10 humans can close 30-50 in 3 months)
5. **Social proof:** Build case studies with first 20 (then use in Phase 2)
