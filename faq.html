General

What is NetWebMedia?

NetWebMedia is an AI-powered marketing agency serving businesses in the United States and Chile. We combine autonomous AI agents, human senior strategists, and a full-stack service suite — CRM, websites, short-form video, SEO, paid ads, social media, and fractional CMO — all under one monthly retainer.

Think of us as your entire marketing department, powered by AI, at a fraction of the cost of building it in-house.

Who are your typical clients?

Our clients range from startups and SMBs looking to grow fast, to established companies wanting to modernize their marketing stack. We work across industries — healthcare, real estate, e-commerce, professional services, restaurants, SaaS, and more.

Common profile: a business doing $500K–$10M+ in annual revenue that wants serious marketing without a $20K/mo agency retainer.

Are you a real agency or just AI tools?

Both — in the best possible way. Every service is human-designed and AI-accelerated. AI handles research, drafting, scheduling, reporting, and optimization. Humans handle strategy, creative direction, quality review, and client relationships.

You get the speed and scale of AI with the judgment and accountability of experienced marketers.

Do you serve both the US and Chile?

Yes. We are fully bilingual (English / Spanish), operate across time zones, and have deep experience with both markets — including US ad platforms, Chilean consumer behavior, Mercado Pago integration, and local SEO in both countries.

Our team is distributed remotely across the Americas, so there is no latency in communication.

What languages do you work in?

English and Spanish, natively. All deliverables — websites, ad copy, SEO articles, social posts, reports — can be produced in either language or both simultaneously. For bilingual markets (US Hispanic, Chilean), we build content strategies that speak to both audiences authentically.

Is there a physical office?

We are remote-first by design. Our team collaborates across the US and Latin America. This lets us operate leaner, serve clients in any timezone, and pass those savings on to you through lower pricing. All client communication happens via your dedicated dashboard, Slack, email, and scheduled video calls.

Pricing & Plans

How does your pricing work?

Every plan has two components: a one-time setup fee and a monthly retainer. The setup fee covers onboarding, configuration, and initial asset creation. The monthly retainer covers all ongoing work — content, optimization, reporting, and support.

The setup fee reflects the real cost of doing it right the first time: technical audits, account configuration, strategy development, brand asset creation, CRM onboarding, and training. AI Services

What AI tools and models do you use?

Our core stack is built on Anthropic's Claude (AI Fractional CMO agent), GPT-4 for certain content workflows, Gemini for AEO and search optimization, Heygen + Higgsfield for AI video avatars, and Seedance for motion content. We combine these with proprietary automation pipelines built on n8n and Make.

We select the best model for each task rather than being locked into one platform.

How are AI chat agents trained on my business?

We build a knowledge base from your website, FAQs, product/service documents, pricing, and any materials you provide. The agent is then tuned via prompt engineering and tested with real customer scenarios before going live. We also set up escalation paths so the agent hands off complex cases to your human team seamlessly.

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

AEO is the practice of structuring your content so AI engines cite your brand when answering user questions — in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE, Gemini, and similar tools. As consumers increasingly ask AI instead of searching Google, being cited in those answers is the new front page ranking.

Our SEO Pro and SEO Max plans include dedicated AEO content and schema markup.

Will AI replace my marketing team?

AI replaces repetitive tasks, not people. Our model is: AI handles research, drafting, scheduling, monitoring, and reporting — humans handle strategy, creative judgment, relationship management, and anything requiring deep context. If you have an in-house team, AI makes them dramatically more productive. If you do not, we become your team.

Can AI agents book appointments and qualify leads automatically?

Yes. Our AI chat agents integrate with calendar tools (Google Calendar, Calendly) for automatic booking, and with your CRM to log contacts, tag lead quality, and trigger follow-up sequences. The agent qualifies leads 24/7 — including nights, weekends, and holidays — so your sales team only deals with warm prospects.

Do you use our data to train AI models?

No. We use enterprise API tiers (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google) that contractually exclude customer data from model training. Your prompts, documents, and conversations are not used to improve the foundational models — they are used only to generate the immediate response and are retained per our data retention policy (30 days for safety review, then deleted).

How do you ensure quality on AI-generated output?

Every AI-generated asset goes through human review before delivery:

  • Content is fact-checked against your knowledge base
  • Brand voice is validated by a senior editor
  • Campaigns are approved by a strategist before launch
  • All claims referencing statistics or external facts are sourced

AI is never autopilot. It is a productivity multiplier behind a human quality gate.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Organic search is a compounding investment. Expect initial ranking movement in 30–60 days for lower-competition keywords, measurable traffic growth by month 3, and significant authority and lead flow by months 6–12. The more consistent the content, the faster the compounding. We recommend SEO as a 6-month minimum for best ROI.

Every article includes:

  • On-page optimization (title, meta, headings, internal linking)
  • AEO schema markup (FAQ, HowTo, Article structured data)
  • Featured image + alt text
  • CMS upload — we publish directly to your site
Do you write content in Spanish?

Yes — all content plans include Spanish-language content on request at no extra cost. We also build bilingual content strategies for brands targeting both English and Spanish speakers simultaneously, including proper hreflang implementation for international SEO.

Do you do backlink building?

SEO Pro and SEO Max plans include active outreach for 3–5 quality backlinks per month. We focus on editorial links from relevant domains (DR 30+) — no PBNs, no link farms, no schemes that could trigger Google penalties. Quality over quantity, always.

Do you optimize for local SEO?

Yes. Local SEO includes:

  • Google Business Profile optimization and weekly posts
  • Local citations on 50+ directories (Yelp, Bing Places, Apple Maps, etc.)
  • NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) across the web

We optimize for US metro markets and Chilean cities (Santiago, Viña del Mar, Concepción, etc.).

Will my site get Google penalties from AI content?

No. Google's spam policy explicitly allows AI-generated content that is helpful, original, and demonstrates E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness). Google's own words: "appropriate use of AI or automation is not against our guidelines."

Our editorial process ensures every article meets those standards: human review, fact-checking, original angles, unique data/examples, and schema markup. Sites using our SEO service routinely rank in the top 3 for competitive keywords.

A white-label reseller program that lets agencies, consultants, and fractional CMOs resell our AI CRM, CMS, Email, Video Factory, and Fractional CMO products under their own brand. You get full platform access under your domain and logo; your clients never see NetWebMedia. Starting at $249/mo. See the full Partner Program →

Which products can be white-labeled?

All five core products:

  • CRM (from $347/mo) — unlimited sub-accounts
  • CMS (from $499/mo) — unlimited client sites
  • Email Marketing (from $249/mo) — dedicated sending domains
  • Video Factory (from $597/mo) — AI video production
  • AI Fractional CMO (from $399/mo) — branded strategy agent
  • Full-Stack Bundle (from $1,499/mo) — all products, most popular
What margins can I make as a partner?

Typical reseller margins are 50–70%. Example: buy the Full-Stack Bundle at $1,499/mo, resell to 10 clients at $2,997/mo = $28,471/mo gross margin. Use the margin calculator to model your own pricing. Many of our partners reach 80%+ margins by bundling additional services (ad management, strategy, content) on top of the platform.

Is there a minimum commitment for partners?

90-day minimum on any white-label tier, then month-to-month with 30 days written notice. This gives you runway to land your first clients. Annual prepay saves 10%. No per-client minimums — you pay only your platform fee whether you have 1 client or 100.

How long does partner onboarding take?

3–7 days from signed agreement to live instance. Includes: white-label domain setup, logo and branding customization, sub-account provisioning flow, partner Slack channel, sales enablement pack (decks, scripts, ROI calculators), and a 45-minute onboarding call. Many partners close their first client within 14 days of going live.

Who handles client billing — me or NetWebMedia?

You do. You bill your clients directly on your invoice, at your price, using whatever payment processor you prefer (Stripe, Square, QuickBooks, Mercado Pago, etc.). We invoice you monthly for the white-label platform. Your clients are entirely your relationship — we stay invisible unless you explicitly bring us in for escalations.

What support do I get as a partner?

Every partner gets: private Slack channel with product + engineering, partner enablement pack (sales decks, pitch scripts, ROI calculators, co-branded case study templates), and priority technical support. Full-Stack partners also get a dedicated partner success manager and quarterly strategy calls.

Can I customize the white-label platform for my clients?

Yes. Domain, logo, color palette, email sender addresses, and preference center are customizable per client. Custom pipelines, automations, and content templates can be pre-configured as your agency defaults. Deeper customizations (custom features, integrations) are available via our API or as scoped professional services.

Do you offer revenue-share models for high-volume partners?

For partners managing 50+ clients, we offer custom revenue-share structures with lower platform costs in exchange for minimum commitments. This is case-by-case — contact us with your projected volume and we'll design terms around your model.

Email Marketing Platform

Is email included with NWM CRM, or is it a separate product?

Both. NWM CRM includes basic email campaigns (templates, broadcast, tracking). For advanced features — AI-written campaigns, behavioral automations, predictive segmentation, SMS/WhatsApp, deliverability management, A/B testing — you'll want NWM Email, available standalone (from $99/mo) or bundled with CRM at a discount.

How is email marketing priced?

By list size, not email volume. Starter $99/mo (up to 2,500 contacts), Growth $249/mo (up to 25,000 contacts), Scale $599/mo (unlimited). Unlimited sends on all plans. Setup fees are one-time and cover domain authentication, list import, and brand kit setup.

How does NWM Email compare to Klaviyo and Mailchimp?

Comparable feature depth, substantially cheaper at the same contact tier. Klaviyo starts around $60/mo at 2,500 contacts but SMS, predictive segmentation, and advanced features each add $150+/mo. NWM Email includes all of that plus WhatsApp, white-label for agencies, AI-written campaigns, and native CRM integration — for $99/mo starter, $249/mo growth.

What kinds of automations can I build?

Welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, winback, re-engagement, birthday, upsell, cross-sell, onboarding drips. Triggered on any event in your CRM, website, Shopify, or Stripe. Visual flow builder, no code needed. AI can draft the entire sequence from a one-sentence brief.

How do you handle deliverability?

DKIM, SPF, and DMARC are configured during onboarding (you add a few DNS records, we handle the rest). Gmail/Yahoo 2024 bulk sender rules are enforced by default. We monitor sender reputation 24/7, alert on bounce spikes, and provide dedicated IPs on Growth + Scale plans. Aggregate inbox placement across all sending domains is 99.3%.

Does email include A/B testing?

Yes, on all plans. Test subject lines, preview text, send times, content variants, and CTAs. Our AI can auto-declare winners based on statistical significance — no manual review needed. Growth + Scale plans include multivariate testing and AI-driven send-time optimization per contact.

Can I send SMS and WhatsApp from the same platform?

Yes. Growth + Scale plans include native SMS + WhatsApp channels. Same segments, same automations, same analytics — unified across email, SMS, and WhatsApp. Contacts can set channel preferences in the branded preference center. SMS requires a dedicated phone number ($2–5/mo) and messaging credits (~$0.008 per US SMS).

Can I migrate from Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or ActiveCampaign?

Yes. We migrate contacts, segments, templates, automations, and historical event data from any major email platform. Starter plan: self-serve CSV import. Growth plan: white-glove migration included in setup. Scale plan: full migration handled by our team with zero downtime for your sends.

Can agencies white-label NWM Email?

Yes. White-label is available from $249/mo through our Partner Program. Your clients see your brand, your domain, your support emails. You manage their email campaigns from a unified dashboard and bill them on your invoice at your own margin.
