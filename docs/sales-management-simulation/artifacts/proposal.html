1 · What we discussed (discovery call, 7 May)

Test Law Group is a 4-attorney firm in the U.S. handling personal-injury and employment matters. The firm runs roughly 12 qualified inquiries/month, converting ~18% to signed retainers. Three bottlenecks surfaced on the call:

  1. Intake latency. First-touch response averages 4–6 hours, well past the 5-minute window that drives 21× conversion lift in legal.
  2. Practice-area discoverability. Test Law Group does not currently rank in the top 10 ChatGPT citations for its two anchor practice areas in its market.
  3. Referral nurture is ad-hoc. No systematic touchpoint with referring attorneys between cases.
