This Engagement Letter ("Letter") confirms the terms under which NetWebMedia SpA ("NWM") will provide AI-Native Fractional CMO services to Test Law Group ("Client") effective 11 May 2026.

1 · Scope of Services

NWM will deliver the CMO Growth service package as defined in Proposal CMO-2026-0042 dated 9 May 2026, including: monthly written CMO reports, AEO citation strategy, 8 AI-content pieces per month, niche landing page maintenance, intake automation, WhatsApp business setup, paid ads management, email automation, and full access to the NetWebMedia CRM platform. The Proposal is incorporated by reference and forms part of this Letter.

2 · Fees & Payment

  1. Monthly retainer: USD $999, billed in advance on the 11th of each month via Stripe.
  2. One-time setup fee: USD $499, billed with the first invoice.
  3. Paid advertising spend is passed through at cost. NWM charges a 12% management fee on ad spend ($300/month minimum).
  4. Annual pre-pay discount available at any time: 12 months for $10,190 (15% savings).

3 · Term & Termination

This engagement has a 90-day minimum commitment. After day 90, either party may terminate with fourteen (14) days' written notice. Client may not terminate during the 90-day minimum except for material breach as defined below.

4 · Founding Client Guarantee

If, during the first 30 days, NWM does not deliver the minimum qualified leads agreed in writing during onboarding, NWM will refund the first monthly retainer ($999). The setup fee is non-refundable. This guarantee is the Client's sole and exclusive remedy for shortfall in the 30-day window.

5 · Intellectual Property

All deliverables created by NWM specifically for Client (landing pages, copy, ad creative, email templates) become Client's property upon full payment. NWM retains ownership of its CRM platform, AI prompts, internal methodology, and any tool or framework used to produce deliverables. NWM may use anonymized performance data ("we helped a personal-injury firm in the Midwest…") for marketing without naming Client.

6 · Confidentiality

Each party will hold the other's confidential information in strict confidence and use it only for the purpose of this engagement. The mutual NDA dated 9 May 2026 is incorporated by reference.

7 · Limitation of Liability

NWM's aggregate liability under this Letter will not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the three (3) months preceding the claim. Neither party will be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

8 · Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Letter is governed by the laws of the Republic of Chile. Any dispute that cannot be resolved through good-faith negotiation within 30 days will be resolved by binding arbitration administered by the International Centre for Dispute Resolution (ICDR) under its International Arbitration Rules. Arbitration will take place in English, with proceedings conducted virtually unless both parties agree otherwise.

9 · Entire Agreement

This Letter, together with Proposal CMO-2026-0042 and the mutual NDA dated 9 May 2026, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
