FAQ

Premium answers, before you book the fit call.

What does CMO Premium include that Growth doesn't?

On top of everything in CMO Growth, Premium adds: 16 short-form videos per month + 2 YouTube long-form videos, multi-channel paid ads including TikTok, a dedicated account strategist (not shared), one custom AI agent built for your business, voice AI for inbound and outbound calls, white-glove onboarding, priority support via Slack, and a lower 10% ads management fee (vs 12% on Growth).

Why is CMO Premium $2,490/mo if a US CMO costs $300k+ a year?

Because we're not competing on labor; we're competing on output. AI agents do the production work, supervised by human strategists. The team that would cost $25,000–$40,000/month to assemble in-house in the US becomes a $2,490/mo retainer because the leverage is software, not headcount.

Is the strategy call really required?

Yes. Premium isn't self-service. We require a 20-minute fit call before accepting any Premium client so we can confirm the engagement makes sense for both sides — scope, industry fit, expected outcomes, and capacity. We turn down accounts that aren't a fit.

What's the dedicated account strategist actually responsible for?

One named human owns your account. They write your monthly strategy report, review every paid ad before it launches, run the monthly priority Slack thread, and are the escalation point if anything goes wrong. They're not a customer success manager — they're a marketer.

What does the custom AI agent do?

One custom AI agent built specifically for your business workflow — for example, an AI receptionist that books appointments, an AI quote builder for service businesses, or an AI concierge for hotels. Built on the NetWebMedia agent stack, owned by you for as long as you stay on Premium.

What does the voice AI cover?

Inbound call answering, qualification, and routing during off-hours, plus outbound lead follow-up calls. US English voice, configurable script, full transcript logged to your CRM. Standalone voice AI vendors charge $800–$2,000/mo for this — it's included in Premium.

What's the Founding Client guarantee on Premium?

Same as Growth: a minimum number of qualified leads in the first 30 days, agreed during onboarding. Miss the threshold, full first-month refund. The threshold on Premium is higher because the scope is bigger.

Can I get more than Premium?

Yes — Custom / Multi-brand. If you operate multiple brands, need custom SLAs, want unlimited video production, or need multiple custom AI agents, we scope a Custom retainer after a strategy call. Pricing is bespoke and starts above Premium.
