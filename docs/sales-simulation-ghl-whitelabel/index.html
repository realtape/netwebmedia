Why GHL is the second-best entry point (after AEO Audit)

  1. Self-qualifying buyer. They want a CRM, not advice. They already know the problem they're solving.
  2. Fast cycle. Most deals close in 1–3 days from demo to provisioned.
  3. Predictable MRR. $197 × every sub-account = the most reliable monthly line on the P&L.
  4. Path to upgrade. ~15% of GHL buyers upgrade to CMO Standard within 6 months once they realize they want done-for-you, not just the tool.
  5. Operational simplicity. One snapshot (Estetica.Social), one provisioning script, predictable support burden.
