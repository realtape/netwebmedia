# NetWebMedia Operations Playbook

## Recommended MVP stack

- Website: static site now, production-ready to move into Next.js/Vercel if needed
- Product builds: Next.js, Node.js, Supabase/Firebase depending on project scope
- CRM: HubSpot Starter or Pipedrive
- Scheduling: Calendly
- Analytics: GA4 + platform-native ad reporting
- Automation: Zapier or Make
- Reporting: Looker Studio or an agency dashboard layer

## Minimum client lifecycle

1. Lead captured
2. Qualified
3. Discovery call
4. Proposal sent
5. Onboarding
6. Launch
7. Weekly optimization
8. Monthly reporting

## SOP: discovery

- Review intake form fields before the call
- Identify whether the need is SaaS, app, CRM, web design, marketing, or a combined engagement
- Confirm budget, timeline, geography, and success criteria
- End with a recommended engagement model and next-step commitment

## SOP: proposal creation

- Restate business goal and bottleneck
- Show engagement scope tied to outcome
- Define first 30-day deliverables
- Clarify KPI baseline, reporting cadence, and client responsibilities
- Include project phase, retainer/support phase when relevant, and optional add-ons

## SOP: onboarding checklist

- Signed agreement
- Billing setup
- Access to website, product repositories, analytics, ad accounts, CRM, and calendar
- Brand inputs and offer details
- Sales contact / closer details
- KPI baseline documented
- Launch timeline confirmed

## SOP: campaign and system launch

- Confirm deliverables, environments, and launch checklist
- Validate tracking and conversion events where relevant
- Test CRM field mapping and notifications
- Check booking flow and follow-up messages where relevant
- Confirm internal owner for launch and client communication

## SOP: weekly optimization

- Review delivery progress, lead quality, and cost signals
- Identify friction in product usage, page conversion, or follow-up
- Adjust priorities, messaging, automation, or qualification logic
- Record decisions and next experiments

## SOP: monthly reporting

- Summarize lead volume and quality
- Compare against agreed KPI targets
- Surface top lessons and bottlenecks
- Recommend next month’s priorities

## v1 automations

- Form submission -> CRM record created
- Form submission -> internal notification sent
- High-score lead -> immediate outreach task
- Discovery booked -> onboarding checklist task created
- Month-end -> reporting reminder created

## What stays manual in v1

- Final proposal writing
- Discovery call delivery
- KPI interpretation and strategic recommendations
- Final creative approval
- Complex CRM branching logic
