1. Which channel, when

For people already searching for what you sell. High buying intent.

Broad reach. Great for retargeting past visitors and finding new customers with creative.

Expensive but precise. Great for business-to-business deals over $10,000.

3. Creative — the biggest lever

90% of ad performance comes from the creative itself, not the targeting. Here is our creative pipeline:

  1. Weekly brief — 5 new ad ideas, different hooks, formats, and angles.
  2. AI-assisted production — stock footage plus AI video plus human editing.
  3. Test with small budget — $50/day per ad for 3 days.
  4. Scale the winners — ads beating the average cost per customer by 30% or more get more budget.
  5. Kill the losers fast — ads at 1.5 times the average cost get paused on day 4.

4. How we split the budget

  • 70% — winners (campaigns that made money in the last 30 days)
  • 20% — scale tests (adding budget to recent winners to find the ceiling)
  • 10% — experiments (new channels, new formats, new angles)

5. Our Monday review

Every Monday at 10am in your time zone. 30-minute call. Agenda:

  • The week at a glance: spend, cost per customer, return on ad spend (every $1 in, how much came back), sales pipeline impact.
  • Top 3 ads of the week and why they worked.
  • Top 3 losers and what we're replacing them with.
  • Next week's creative brief and budget changes.
  • Any red flags (tired audiences, policy issues, platform problems).

6. Proving what actually worked

Meta says it drove X. Google says Y. Both over-claim. We triangulate:

  • Google Analytics — baseline truth across all channels.
  • Post-purchase survey — a single question at checkout: "Where did you hear about us?"
  • Turn-off tests — quarterly, we pause a channel in one region and measure the drop.
  • Full media modeling — on accounts spending over $50,000/month.

What success looks like

  • Within 2 weeks: winning ads start to emerge from the test group.
  • Within 1 month: return on ad spend is stable. You know your cost per customer.
  • Within 3 months: winners are scaled up, losers are killed, and you're getting a higher return than when you started.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Running one ad forever. Audiences get tired fast. Fresh creative every week or performance sinks.
  • Obsessing over clicks. Clicks are cheap. Customers are what count. Track both, reward the second.
  • Trusting the platform's own numbers. Meta and Google both over-claim. Always check against Google Analytics and your CRM.
