# Offer and Pricing

## Core services

NetWebMedia launches with five core services:

- SaaS development
- App development
- Digital marketing
- CRM setup and automation
- Web design

These services can be sold as standalone engagements or combined into a build-plus-growth roadmap.

## Commercial model

1. Initial scoped project
2. Optional monthly optimization or support retainer

This structure creates a clear implementation phase followed by ongoing growth, support, or optimization work.

## Fulfillment scope

- SaaS MVP planning and launch support
- App development for customer-facing or internal workflows
- Website design and conversion path implementation
- CRM setup, field design, pipeline logic, and automation
- Digital marketing strategy, campaign setup, and reporting
- Analytics and KPI tracking setup
- Weekly optimization cadence when retained
- Monthly reporting review when retained

## Add-ons

- Paid media management
- SEO expansion pages
- Bilingual funnel adaptation
- Chatbot or AI assistant implementation
- Sales script and follow-up system design

## Packaging guidance

Launch with a clear service menu and package custom scopes after discovery. Introduce fixed tiers only when complexity differs materially by build depth, channel mix, or support needs.

## Price framing

Use custom pricing after discovery for the launch phase. The sales message should emphasize:

- product and growth acceleration
- reduced operational fragmentation
- stronger customer acquisition systems
- increased operational clarity

## Founding-client offer structure

- Limit early pilot slots
- Offer higher strategic access in exchange for feedback and testimonial potential
- Keep scope boundaries clear to avoid underpriced custom work
