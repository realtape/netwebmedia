# HeyGen Brief — CRM Platform Reel

**Use:** HeyGen → "Generate a video from a prompt" (middle-top card on home).
Auto-generates: script + avatar + voiceover + b-roll.

---

## Step 1 — Paste this into the prompt box

```
Create a 30-second vertical (9:16) Instagram Reel for NetWebMedia, an AI-native
marketing agency. This reel promotes our NWM CRM Platform — 46 modules, 7 hubs,
one login. It replaces HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, and 4 other tools at a fraction
of the cost.

TARGET AUDIENCE: Founders, ops leaders, and agency owners paying $300–$1,500/mo
across multiple SaaS tools — HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, Typeform, Calendly, etc.
They're drowning in integrations and want one unified platform. Also relevant to
agencies who want to white-label and resell.

TONE: Clean and confident. The opening hook should feel like a genuine "aha."
No fluff. Lead with the number — 46 modules, one login — then payoff.

VISUAL STYLE: Modern, dark-mode tech aesthetic. Deep navy (#010F3B) and black
backgrounds with electric orange (#FF671F) accents. Bold sans-serif type
overlays. B-roll: iMac screens showing CRM dashboards, professional office
environments, team collaboration settings. Minimal, high-contrast aesthetic.

SCRIPT (use exactly this — do not rewrite):

"Seven hubs. One login.
Your entire marketing stack — CRM, automations, email, AI agents,
and a full CMS — all in one platform.
Forty-six live modules. Replace five tools with one.
And it is fully white-label, so you can resell it under your brand at any price.
Visit netwebmedia dot com."

SHOT-BY-SHOT DIRECTION:
- 0–2s: Talking head, tight crop, direct delivery. "Seven hubs. One login."
  — punchy, like revealing something obvious that nobody did yet.
- 2–9s: B-roll — man at iMac Pro, dark background, smiling while navigating
  a unified dashboard. Screen shows: CRM, Email, Automation, CMS, AI tabs
  all in one interface. Professional, modern.
- 9–12s: B-roll — call-center team with headsets in a modern professional
  office. Shows the platform being used at scale. Multiple screens.
- 12–16s: Quick cuts — 5 competitor logos (HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, Typeform,
  Calendly, Zapier) each with a red X, dissolving into a single orange NWM
  logo. "Replace five tools with one."
- 16–25s: B-roll — creative open office with multiple iMac desktops, team
  reviewing analytics dashboards. White-label resale angle voiced over.
- 25–30s: End card — "netwebmedia.com / Starting at $249/mo" bold type on
  dark navy gradient with orange ring accent.

BURNED CAPTIONS: Yes — 2-3 word phrases, large bold white text with black
outline, lower-middle position, synced to VO. Highlight key words (46 modules,
One login, White-label, $249, netwebmedia.com) in electric orange (#FF671F).

END CTA: "netwebmedia.com — Starting at $249/mo"

MUSIC: Subtle lo-fi tech pulse, 90 BPM, mixed at -18 dB under voiceover.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920, MP4, 30fps, H.264, under 30 seconds.
```
