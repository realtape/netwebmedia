# WhatsApp + Email Outreach Sequences
## Warm Leads (Post-Form Submission) by Niche

**Context:** Each sequence fires when a prospect submits a landing page form. WhatsApp message 1 goes out within 5 minutes. Email sends same day. WhatsApp message 2 follows 24 hours later if no reply.

**Voice:** Direct, value-first, async-friendly. Respect their time. No Zoom push. Pain → impact.

---

## NICHE 1: LAW FIRMS

**Positioning angle:** "You're invisible in AI search where your clients are asking legal questions. We get you cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI in 90 days."

**Pain points:**
- Law firms spend on SEO but new clients ask ChatGPT, not Google
- Marketing is scattered (website, blog, social, referrals) with no one connecting the dots
- They can't afford a full-time CMO; fractional fits perfectly for retainer-based practices
- Compliance-conscious; need someone who won't overpromise

**Economic buyer:** Managing partner or practice leader (decision = $3k-$5k/month retainer)
**Champion:** Office manager or marketing committee member (brings it to the partner)
**Decision timeline:** Usually 4-6 weeks (partner consensus culture)

---

### WhatsApp Message 1 (within 5 min of form submit)
**Max 3 sentences. Hook = AI visibility gap.**

```
Hi [First Name], thanks for checking us out. Quick question: when someone asks ChatGPT "best employment attorney near [city]," does your firm show up? Most law firms are invisible in AI search. We specialize in fixing that. Let's find 15 min this week?
```

**Why this works:**
- Opens with a specific, painful question (ChatGPT visibility)
- Frames the core pain in 1 sentence
- Soft CTA (not "book a demo," just "15 min")
- Respects async nature of law biz (no pressure to reply instantly)

---

### WhatsApp Message 2 (24h later, if no reply)
**Max 2 sentences. Hook = proof point.**

```
Just following up — our clients average 4.2x more AI citations within 90 days. If visibility in ChatGPT matters for your practice, let me know and I'll share a quick teardown of [law firm competitor] we worked with.
```

**Why this works:**
- Leads with a number (proof, not hype)
- Names a competitor (creates FOMO gently)
- Offers a free audit as the next step (low friction)

---

### Email (sent same day as WhatsApp 1)
**Subject line:** "Your firm + ChatGPT: the visibility gap"

```
Hi [First Name],

Your practice page is sharp. But I noticed you're not showing up in ChatGPT when prospects ask "best [practice area] attorney in [city]." That's where your best leads are starting now.

We help law firms get cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Strategy + content + automation, all in one monthly retainer. No long-term agency commitment. Most practices see 4.2x more AI citations in 90 days.

Quick next step: I can do a 5-minute teardown of your current AI visibility vs. a competitor in your market. Takes 15 min, no Zoom needed.

Two options:
1. Reply "audit" and I'll send the findings by end of week
2. Pick a 15-min slot this week: [calendar link]

Either way, we'll know what's possible.

Thanks,
[Your name]
NetWebMedia
```

**Stats:**
- Word count: ~120
- CTA framing: choice architecture (audit OR calendar), not binary "book now"
- Specific pain: ChatGPT visibility for law firms
- No discount mention (brand guardrail)
- Proof point: 4.2x citation lift

## NICHE 2: HEALTH (Dental, Medical, Therapy, Wellness)

**Positioning angle:** "Your patients are asking Google and ChatGPT about treatments before they book. You need to be the answer."

**Pain points:**
- Health practitioners lose leads at the research stage (prospects shop AI before calling)
- They need credibility in AI search (trust = citations from trusted sources)
- Limited budget (many are solo/small group); they won't pay $500+/month for generic marketing
- Compliance matters (HIPAA-aware, can't be too salesy)

**Economic buyer:** Practice owner or office manager
**Champion:** Whoever handles patient intake/booking
**Decision timeline:** 2-3 weeks (smaller practices move faster)

---

### WhatsApp Message 1 (within 5 min of form submit)
**Max 3 sentences. Hook = patient research stage.**

```
Hi [First Name], just saw you filled out the form. Here's the thing: your patients are asking ChatGPT "[treatment] vs [treatment]" or "best [specialty] near me" BEFORE they call you. Most practices aren't showing up in those answers. We fix that. Can we talk 15 min this week?
```

**Why this works:**
- Opens with a patient journey insight (not a marketing lecture)
- Specific to healthcare (ChatGPT treatment research)
- Frames "invisibility" as a patient acquisition gap
- Easy rhythm (casual, not corporate)

---

### WhatsApp Message 2 (24h later, if no reply)
**Max 2 sentences. Hook = trust + proof.**

```
One more thing — we worked with a [specialty] practice in [nearby city] who went from 0 AI citations to 127 in 60 days. If that interests you, I've got a 10-min breakdown to share.
```

**Why this works:**
- Specific, measurable result (127 citations, not "lots")
- References a local competitor/similar practice (credibility)
- Offer is free and fast (10 min breakdown, not a 30-min sales call)

---

### Email (sent same day as WhatsApp 1)
**Subject line:** "Where your patients are asking about [treatment]"

```
Hi [First Name],

Your practice looks great online. But here's what's happening behind the scenes: patients ask ChatGPT about their symptoms or treatment options, and you're not in the answer.

We help health practices show up in AI search—the place where your patients are actually looking. We handle the strategy and content so you can focus on care.

One retainer covers everything: website optimization, AI-friendly content, local visibility, the works. No long contract. Most practices see results in 60 days.

Next step is simple. I can:

1. Send you a free audit of your current AI visibility (takes 5 min to read)
2. Or we schedule 15 min this week to talk specifics

Reply "audit" or grab a slot here: [calendar link]

Quick question for you: what's the #1 reason new patients don't book with you?

[Your name]
NetWebMedia
```

**Stats:**
- Word count: ~130
- Patient-first framing (not practice-first)
- Proof: "60 days" is faster than law firms, fits healthcare urgency
- Soft CTA with choice (audit OR call)
- Discovery Q at the end (preps them for the next conversation)

## NICHE 3: RESTAURANTS

**Positioning angle:** "Your diners research on Google and ChatGPT before they book. Be the answer they see first."

**Pain points:**
- Restaurants can't afford $3k+/month agencies but need online visibility to fill tables
- Their economic model is tight; CAC matters more than any other business
- They need quick wins (not 90-day journeys); they live month-to-month
- Marketing knowledge is low; they trust word-of-mouth, not strategy
- Bilingual priority (LATAM + US Latino markets)

**Economic buyer:** Owner or GM
**Champion:** Front-of-house manager or whoever runs social
**Decision timeline:** 1-2 weeks (super fast; they make gut calls)

---

### WhatsApp Message 1 (within 5 min of form submit)
**Max 3 sentences. Hook = "diners are asking their phone, not calling you."**

```
Hey [First Name], thanks for checking us out. Real talk: diners are asking Google and ChatGPT "best [cuisine] restaurant in [city]" before they call. If you're not in the top answer, you're losing seats every night. We fix that. Free 10-min breakdown?
```

**Why this works:**
- Conversational tone ("Real talk," "Hey")
- Frames the problem in their language (losing seats = lost revenue)
- "Free 10-min breakdown" is ultra-light friction
- Urgency (losing seats NOW, not in 90 days)

---

### WhatsApp Message 2 (24h later, if no reply)
**Max 2 sentences. Hook = speed + result.**

```
We helped a [cuisine] spot in [nearby area] go from 12 to 38 AI citations in 45 days. More citations = more bookings. Worth a quick look?
```

**Why this works:**
- Hyper-specific result (45 days, 12→38 citations)
- Local proof (nearby area = relevant)
- Connects citations to outcome they care about (bookings = seats = $)

---

```

Your restaurant looks amazing. But your diners are asking ChatGPT "best [cuisine] near [area]" and you're not showing up.

Simple retainer. No long contract. No fluff. We handle the strategy; you run the kitchen.

Most restaurants see a boost in visibility within 30 days.

Quick next step:

**Option 1:** I'll send you a free 5-minute breakdown of your current AI visibility vs. the top competitor nearby
**Option 2:** We grab 15 min this week (no sales pitch, just answering questions)

Reply "breakdown" or book here: [calendar link]

—
[Your name]
NetWebMedia

P.S. – We're bilingual (EN/ES) and work with restaurants across the US and LATAM.
```

**Stats:**
- Word count: ~130
- Speed-focused language ("30 days," not "90 days")
- Outcome-driven ("more seats filled")
- PS adds bilingual credibility (nod to LATAM market)
- Ultra-simple CTA (breakdown OR call, binary choice)
- No mention of "agency" or "CMO" (restaurants don't relate to that language)

## Cross-Niche Delivery Notes

1. **WhatsApp timing:** 5 min after form submit (automation via Zapier/Make or manual if volume is low)
2. **Email timing:** Same day, within 2-4 hours of form submit
3. **Follow-up 24h after WhatsApp 1:** Only if no reply. No third message after that (respect bandwidth).
4. **Discovery audit offer:** Each niche gets a customized "free teardown" that references a local competitor or nearby example. Build these as simple 1-pagers, not 20-slide decks.
5. **Calendar links:** Use Calendly or similar; default to 15 min slots (law), 15 min slots (health), 10-15 min (restaurants). No meeting prep required from prospect.
6. **Bilingual override for restaurants:** Consider Spanish-language versions for geo-targeted LATAM sequences (same structure, tone shifts per Brand Book §15).

## Message Template Variables (for personalization)

Replace these in each outreach:

- `[First Name]` — from form submission
- `[city]` — prospect's location (from form or IP detection)
- `[cuisine]` or `[specialty]` or `[practice area]` — inferred from niche + industry research
- `[treatment]` — relevant to healthcare niche (e.g., "wisdom tooth extraction," "couples therapy")
- `[law firm competitor]` / `[nearby practice]` / `[cuisine spot]` — local competitor discovered via research (use real examples, no made-up names)
- `[calendar link]` — Calendly or booking URL, set to 15-min slots
- `[Your name]` — signature; consider adding "Head of Sales, NetWebMedia" for credibility

## Why No LinkedIn or Cold Calls

**Brand guardrail (from Marketing Plan, section 03):**
- LinkedIn: $0 budget, CEO directive
- Phone: Async-first culture; WhatsApp + email = faster response + less friction

**Prospect behavior:** Warm leads (form submitters) are already interested. They're reading async. Respecting that mindset = higher trust, faster close.

## Bilingual Note (Restaurants + LATAM Health)

For Spanish-language sequences:

- **Spanish tone shifts per Brand Book §15:** Usted (formal) for business owners, Caribbean/LATAM Spanish (not Spain)
- **Humidity reduced slightly in Spanish** (directness translates as brusque; soften "losing seats" to "más reservas posibles")
- **Pricing always in USD** in sequences (adjust only in geo-targeted paid ads)
- **Example ES opening for restaurants:**

```
Hola [Nombre], gracias por visitarnos. Aquí va: tus clientes preguntan a ChatGPT "mejores [tipo cocina] cerca de [ciudad]" antes de llamarte. Si no apareces en la respuesta, pierdes reservas cada noche. Te ayudamos a aparecer. Algo en 10 min?
```

**Key difference:** "pierdes reservas cada noche" (lose reservations every night) instead of "losing seats" — more culturally resonant in LATAM hospitality.
