## Strategic Content Map (What to Publish)

### Vertical Case Studies (14 videos, 8–12 min each)
One video per industry. Format: **Problem → NetWebMedia Solution → Results → How to Get Started.**

Example: "Restaurants: How AI Automates Customer Retention Without Email Burnout"
- Problem: 40% of restaurant customers never come back. Email fatigue kills engagement.
- Solution: NetWebMedia fractional CMO + AI segmentation + automated win-back campaigns.
- Data point: "Our restaurant clients see 24% increase in repeat bookings in 60 days."
- CTA: "Get a free vertical audit for your restaurant."

**Why this works for AEO:** Queries like "how to automate restaurant marketing," "best CRM for restaurants," "email marketing for restaurants" will cite these videos. 14 verticals = 14 anchor points in AI Overviews.

**Timeline:** 1 per week = 14 weeks. 8–12 min = long-form = 94% of citation pool.

### Founder/Behind-the-Scenes (6 videos, 12–15 min each)
Adapt plan docs into narrative video essays. Series: "How We Built a $X MRR AI Agency in Chile."

Examples:
- "Our 90-Day Execution Plan: What Actually Worked for Growing from 0 to $50K MRR"
- "The Business Model That Scales: Why Fractional CMO + AI Beats Agency"
- "Marketing Plan 2026: Where We're Betting on AI (and Where We're Not)"

**Why this works:** Founder commentary is 2nd-order AEO play. Queries like "how to start an AI marketing agency," "fractional CMO business model," "AI agency strategy" pull videos from founders who show work transparently.

**Timeline:** 1 per month = 6 videos. 12–15 min = research material for marketing founders building similar businesses. High citation likelihood because niche-specific.

### Product Walkthroughs (4 videos, 6–8 min each)
"CRM for AI Agencies: 4 Features You Need in 2026"
- Live demo of crm-vanilla: email automation, lead scoring, campaign templates.
- Comparison angle: "Why we built our own CRM instead of HubSpot for agencies."
- Queries: "Best CRM for AI agencies," "How to automate lead capture," "Email marketing CRM features."

**Timeline:** 1 per quarter (quarterly product update cycle). 6–8 min = searchable demo.
