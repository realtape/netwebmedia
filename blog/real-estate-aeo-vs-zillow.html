Real Estate AEO vs. Zillow: Which Generates Better Leads in 2026

By Carlos Martínez · May 13, 2026 · 8 min read

You pay Zillow $15 per lead. 100 Zillow leads = $1,500, with a 3–6% conversion rate to closed deals. Three converted deals at $12,000 commission each = $36,000 gross, or $34,500 net after Zillow fees. That's not bad. But a real estate agent publishing 12 neighborhood guides in AEO format sees 400–600 website visits per month, converts 8–14 of them to buyer representation, and pays $0 per lead. That same three-deal outcome costs $0 in lead fees. The difference between AEO and Zillow isn't whether one works — both work. The difference is which one scales without eating your margin.

What Each Channel Actually Costs

Cost Type Zillow AEO
Monthly platform fee $0–$400 (if agent pays for premium) $250–$750 (website + content tools)
Per-lead cost $5–$25 per lead (Zillow controls) $0 per lead (owned channel)
Lead volume (monthly) 20–50 leads 8–18 leads
Conversion rate (lead to deal) 3–6% 8–14%
Cost per closed deal $2,500–$8,300 $180–$600

Why Zillow Leads Underperform

Zillow leads are high-volume commodities. When a buyer clicks "Contact Agent" on Zillow, that lead is often sold to 3–5 agents simultaneously. The buyer hears from multiple agents within 10 minutes, picks the first one to call back, and the other agents waste time chasing dead leads. Zillow lead quality has degraded 35–40% since 2022 because of this saturation (NAR 2025). A Zillow lead in a competitive market (Austin, Denver, Miami) has a 2–4% conversion rate. That same lead outside a competitive market has a 6–10% conversion rate — but you pay the same per-lead fee regardless.

Why AEO Leads Convert Higher

A buyer asking ChatGPT "best real estate agent in Pacific Beach, San Diego" is not comparison shopping. They're ready to work with someone. AEO-sourced leads skip the "tire kicking" phase that Zillow leads live in. They come pre-qualified: they know your name, they've read your neighborhood guides, and they trust your market knowledge before they call. Agents report that AEO leads close 25–30% faster than Zillow leads (40 days vs. 56 days on average) and have 22% higher conversion rate.

The Hidden Cost: Zillow Premier Agent Zone Pricing in 2026

Zillow introduced exclusive "zone" pricing in mid-2025: if you want to be the only agent shown for a specific ZIP code on Zillow, you pay a premium. In competitive markets, that premium is brutal. Denver ZIP 80202? $300–$600/month for exclusive zone placement. San Francisco 94105? $400–$800/month. Los Angeles 90210? $350–$700/month. These are monthly commitments — if you stop paying, another agent takes the zone. Real estate agents in top markets often pay $3,600–$9,600/year for a single ZIP code. By contrast, AEO investment to rank for "best agent in [neighborhood]" on ChatGPT and Perplexity runs $3,000–$8,000/year total, covers unlimited ZIP codes and neighborhoods, and you own the asset. One agent's Zillow zone cost is another agent's entire AEO content library.

The Real Difference: Brand Control

Zillow owns your relationship with buyers. They control the listing presentation, the lead flow timing, the buyer communication flow, and the pricing they charge for leads. You're a vendor. When Zillow raises lead costs by 20% next year, you have no negotiating power. AEO builds a direct relationship with buyers. Your website is your asset. Your neighborhood guides are your content. Your reputation is your brand. Zillow lists your home; AEO positions you as the expert buyers should trust.

The Verdict: Stack Both, Prioritize by Stage

New agents (<$2M/year): 70% Zillow, 30% AEO. You need lead volume fast. Zillow gives it immediately. Use AEO as a long-term brand builder.

Established agents (>$5M/year): 30% Zillow, 70% AEO. You don't need volume. You need quality. AEO gives you 8–14 super-qualified leads per month at 8–14× lower cost per deal.

Scaling phase (aim at $10M/year): Start transitioning from 50/50 Zillow/AEO to 20/80, as your AEO content library grows. Zillow is a lead faucet for now. AEO is the asset you own forever.
