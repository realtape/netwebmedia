## Top 3 AEO Wins to Protect

1. **Homepage title and meta description.** "AI-Native Fractional CMO for US SMBs" is precise, searchable, and matches intent across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude queries about fractional CMO services. Do not change.

2. **robots.txt and llms.txt are correctly configured.** GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Anthropic-ai, all explicitly allowed. This is table-stakes and NetWebMedia has it. Keep it current.

3. **Blog post angles on AEO are contrarian and opinionated.** "AEO Replaces SEO in 2026" and "LLM SEO: Getting Cited" are strong takes that differentiate from vanilla SEO content. These establish authority. Expand this series — don't dilute it with neutral summaries.

## Top 5 Content Improvements (Ranked: Impact ÷ Effort)

### 1. **FAQ Schema on blog posts (High impact, low effort)**
Add explicit FAQPage schema + Question/Answer markup to all blog posts, starting with top 5 (AEO posts, autonomous agents, ChatGPT enterprise). Claude and Perplexity prefer extractable Q&A. Effort: 2 hours per post. Impact: 30–40% increase in citation likelihood. **Do this first.**

### 2. **"NetWebMedia AEO Methodology" — a named framework (High impact, medium effort)**
Your brand has no proprietary name. Competitors have: "Surround Sound SEO" (Omniscient Digital), "Answer Density" (First Page Sage). Create a named 5-step AEO framework (e.g., "Entity, Authority, Clarity, Velocity, Citation"). Publish as a single authoritative post. Make it the one thing people cite when they reference NWM. Effort: 1 week. Impact: Becomes quotable; cited in industry roundups.

### 3. **Original research report on "State of AEO adoption among US SMBs" (High impact, high effort)**
Survey 200–300 US SMBs on AEO adoption, barriers, tools used. Publish findings. Pitch to TechCrunch, CMSWire, HubSpot blog, First Page Sage. One Tier-1 placement = 15–40 earned backlinks, training-data weight. Effort: 4–6 weeks. Impact: Transforms brand from commenter to cited authority.

### 4. **Industry page Q&A + citation hooks (Medium impact, medium effort)**
Each of 14 industry pages needs 3–5 Q&As specific to that vertical (e.g., "How does AEO help law firms get more qualified leads?"). Add schema. Cross-link industry pages to blog. Effort: 3 hours per page × 14 = 42 hours. Impact: Each industry page becomes a minor citation magnet for vertical-specific queries.

### 5. **Blog "related posts" internal linking architecture (Medium impact, low effort)**
Add "Read next" or "See also" section to bottom of every post, linking to 2–3 topically related articles. Create tag clusters (AEO, AI Agents, Fractional CMO). Use descriptive anchor text. Effort: 8 hours (template + mass apply). Impact: Keeps readers on-site longer, signals topical authority to LLMs, improves crawl depth.
