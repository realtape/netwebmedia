🚫 Gap #1: No Website

CRITICAL

What's happening

6 of 40 businesses (15%) have zero web presence — no domain, no landing page, nothing. They rely entirely on Facebook pages, Instagram profiles, or WhatsApp. These include a veterinary hospital, a niche exotic animal clinic, a beauty salon, and three restaurants/cafes.

Why it matters

  • 90% of consumers research a business online before visiting (Google, 2025)
  • 88% of mobile searchers visit or call within 24 hours of a local search
  • Valparaiso receives 3.5M+ tourists annually — they search for restaurants, hotels, and services on Google, not on Facebook
  • AI search engines (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) cannot cite businesses with no indexable web content
  • UNESCO heritage cerros (Alegre, Concepcion, Bellavista) are hyper-competitive for tourist attention — no website means invisible to every international visitor

Valparaiso context

Valparaiso's economy is deeply tourism-dependent. International visitors plan their cerro walks, restaurant reservations, and hostel stays weeks in advance via Google. Businesses with no website are invisible to the entire planning phase. Vina del Mar's beach tourism is equally search-driven — "hotel Renaca", "spa Vina del Mar", "restaurante borde costero" are all high-volume queries with zero results from these 6 businesses.
