1. What's included

2. Plan per platform

LinkedIn

Leadership posts, founder's point of view, customer stories. 3 posts/week. Goal: inbound leads.

Instagram

Brand aesthetic, behind the scenes, short videos. 4 or 5/week. Goal: trust and awareness.

TikTok

Educational, entertaining, jumping on trends. 5/week. Goal: discovery by new audiences.

X / Twitter

Opinions, reacting to news, community conversation. 1 to 3/day. Goal: brand voice and founder presence.

3. How content gets made

1

Monthly async strategy report.

We send you a written review of last month's numbers, upcoming launches, and hot topics in your industry. You reply async — no call needed.

2

Content calendar (delivered in 5 days).

Topics. Formats. Posting dates. Hook lines. You see the whole month laid out.

3

Design and copy.

Brand-matched graphics (built in Figma) plus captions drafted by Content AI. You approve in a shared review doc.

4

Schedule and post.

We schedule everything inside Social. Posts go out at platform-best times. Replies and DMs come back into Conversations.

4. Replying (the part most agencies skip)

Posting without engaging is shouting into a void. Here is what daily engagement looks like:

  • Reply to every direct message and comment within 4 business hours.
  • 15 minutes per day commenting on 10 to 15 target accounts (customers, prospects, peers).
  • Share every mention from happy customers or press.
  • Like, save, and react to relevant content in your feed.
Engagement compounds

Accounts that engage daily grow 3 to 5 times faster than accounts that only post. The algorithm rewards showing up.

5. Reporting

Weekly Friday report:

  • Per platform: posts, reach, engagement, followers up or down.
  • Top 3 posts of the week.
  • Biggest moments (viral replies, DMs from target customers, press pickups).
  • Next week's calendar at a glance.

Monthly deep-dive: a 20-page PDF with trend analysis, competitor benchmarks, and what we recommend changing for next month.

What success looks like

  • Within 30 days: a full content calendar, all 4 accounts posting on schedule, replies going out same day.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Posting the same thing on every platform. Each platform wants a different format. We rewrite per platform.
  • Skipping replies. Posting without replying is the #1 reason accounts stall.
  • Only posting when you're excited. Posting weekly beats posting in bursts. Consistency wins.
