## 2. Market Analysis

### Target Market Sizing

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):**
- Mid-market B2B companies: ~50,000 in US/LATAM
- Spending on digital marketing: $150K-$1M/year
- CMO outsourcing penetration: 12%

**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):**
- High-growth SaaS/tech/fintech: 8,000 companies
- Geographic focus: US + Chile (Spanish-speaking)
- Target revenue: $500K-$2M in marketing

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):**
- 12-month target: 8-12 customers at $30K-$150K ACV
- 24-month target: 30-40 customers

### Market Trends Driving Demand

1. **CMO Shortage** — 30% of mid-market companies lack dedicated CMO
2. **AI Adoption** — Enterprises shifting to automation, creating demand for affordable tools
3. **Remote Work** — Outsourced services normalized post-2020
4. **Data Privacy** — Companies shifting from Google Analytics to privacy-first platforms
5. **Marketing Tech Stack Fragmentation** — Average company uses 50+ tools; demand for integration

## 4. Marketing & Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Channel Strategy (Prioritized)

**1. Outbound Sales (40% of new customer acquisition)**
- LinkedIn/email outreach to VP Marketing, CMOs
- Target: High-intent in-market buyers
- Sales team: 1 SDR (current), +1 AE (hire Q3)
- Cadence: Weekly prospecting, monthly demos
- Target: 2-3 new qualified leads/week

**2. Content & Thought Leadership (25%)**
- Blog: Weekly posts on CMO/marketing strategy
- LinkedIn: Daily insights + case studies
- SEO targets: "CMO services," "marketing automation," "CRM for startups"
- Podcast: Monthly interviews with client CMOs
- Goal: 500 monthly organic leads by Q4

**3. Case Studies & Referrals (20%)**
- 6-8 published case studies highlighting ROI
- Customer referral program: $5K per qualified customer
- Partner programs: Agencies, freelancers
- Goal: 20% of new revenue from referrals by Q4

**4. Paid Digital (10%)**
- Google Ads (branded + high-intent keywords)
- LinkedIn Ads (narrow B2B targeting)
- Budget: $2K/mo, targeting 5:1 ROAS
- Goal: Trial signups, webinar registrations

**5. Events & Community (5%)**
- Host quarterly "CMO Roundtable" webinars
- Attend/sponsor 2-3 industry conferences/year
- Build Slack community for customers
- Goal: Brand awareness + customer stickiness

### Messaging Framework

**For CMO/VP Marketing personas:**
- Problem: Understaffed, outsourcing is risky
- Solution: Full-time CMO team, fractional cost
- Proof: Case studies (Acme Corp +150% lead growth)
- CTA: Free 30-min CMO audit

**For CFO/CEO personas:**
- Problem: Marketing spend inefficient, no accountability
- Solution: Data-driven strategy + transparent reporting
- Proof: Average 3.2x ROAS improvement
- CTA: Free financial model audit
