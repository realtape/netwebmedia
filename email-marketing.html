Email marketing FAQ

Is email marketing included with NWM CRM, or separate?

NWM CRM includes basic email campaigns (templates, broadcast, tracking). For advanced features — AI-written campaigns, behavioral automations, predictive segmentation, SMS/WhatsApp, deliverability management — you'll want NWM Email, which is available as a standalone product or bundled with CRM at a discount.

How many emails can I send per month?

Unlimited sends on all plans. Pricing is based on contact list size, not email volume. Send a campaign 10x to the same list — no extra charge.

Can I migrate from Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or ActiveCampaign?

Yes. We migrate contacts, segments, templates, automations, and historical event data from any major email platform. Starter plan: self-serve CSV import. Growth plan: white-glove migration included in setup. Scale plan: full migration handled by our team, with zero downtime for your sends.

Do you set up DKIM, SPF, and DMARC for me?

Yes — all authentication is configured during onboarding. You add a few DNS records (we send the exact values and instructions), and we handle the rest. Gmail/Yahoo 2024 bulk sender rules are enforced by default.

What about GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and Ley 19.628 compliance?

Built-in. Every email includes a compliant unsubscribe link and preference center. Double opt-in available for GDPR contacts. We provide the physical address footer, manage suppression lists, and handle complaint routing automatically.

Can I use my own domain for sending?

Yes. Growth and Scale plans include sending from your own domain (e.g., hello@yourbrand.com). Starter plan uses a shared sending subdomain with your friendly-from name. Upgrades are instant.

Can agencies white-label NWM Email for clients?

Yes. White-label is available from $249/mo as part of our Partner Program. Your clients see your brand, your domain, your support emails. You manage their email campaigns from a unified dashboard and bill them on your invoice.

What integrations are supported?

Native: Shopify, WooCommerce, Stripe, Calendly, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, NWM CRM, NWM CMS. API + webhook support for custom integrations. Zapier/Make for 3,000+ other apps. WordPress plugin included.

How fast can I send my first campaign?

24-48 hours after signing up. Onboarding includes domain authentication (a few DNS records), list import, and brand kit setup. Many Starter-plan customers send their first campaign same-day using shared sending infrastructure.

What if my deliverability drops?

We monitor reputation 24/7 and alert you to issues before they impact delivery. If bounce rates climb or you land on a blacklist, our team intervenes: IP warming, sender reconfiguration, content analysis, and engagement cleanup. Scale plan includes a 2-hour SLA on deliverability incidents.
