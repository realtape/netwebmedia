## Step 1 — Paste this into the prompt box

```
Create a 30-second vertical (9:16) Instagram Reel for NetWebMedia, an AI-native
marketing agency. This reel promotes our AI Agents service — an always-on AI
sales team (AI SDR, Voice AI, Chat Agent) that works 24/7 without a human
bottleneck.

TARGET AUDIENCE: Founders and sales leaders at SMBs who are paying full-time
reps to handle inbound, losing leads after hours, and still missing quota.

TONE: Confident, direct, founder-to-founder. No hype. Plain English, punchy,
short sentences. The hook should feel like a late-night realization.

VISUAL STYLE: Modern, dark-mode tech aesthetic. Deep navy (#010F3B) and black
backgrounds with electric orange (#FF671F) accents. Bold sans-serif type
overlays. Cinematic b-roll — professional office environments, dual monitors,
real business settings. High contrast. No stock-photo cheese.

SCRIPT (use exactly this — do not rewrite):

"What if your website could close deals while you sleep.
NetWebMedia AI Agents engage every visitor, qualify leads,
and book meetings around the clock.
Our AI SDR.
Our Voice AI.
Your always-on sales team — powered by the latest large language models.
Visit netwebmedia dot com."

SHOT-BY-SHOT DIRECTION:
- 0–4s: Talking head, tight crop, founder speaks directly to camera, navy
  background. Hook line delivery — confident, slightly conspiratorial.
- 4–9s: B-roll — developer at dual desktop monitors in a professional open
  office, screens showing CRM dashboards and chat interfaces. Problem framing.
- 9–14s: Clean product UI mockup b-roll — AI chat widget interface engaging a
  visitor, lead qualification flow shown in navy/orange UI. Solution reveal.
- 14–19s: Quick cuts — three separate full-screen cards each labeled
  "AI SDR", "Voice AI", "Chat Agent" in bold orange on dark navy. Each card
  with a glowing orange icon.
- 19–25s: Talking head returns, confident, minimal modern office background
  with floor-to-ceiling windows. Delivery: "Your always-on sales team."
- 25–30s: End card — "netwebmedia.com / Starting at $249/mo" bold type on
  dark navy gradient with orange ring accent.

BURNED CAPTIONS: Yes — 2-3 word phrases, large bold white text with black
outline, lower-middle position, synced to VO. Highlight key words (AI SDR,
Voice AI, $249, netwebmedia.com) in electric orange (#FF671F).

END CTA: "netwebmedia.com — Starting at $249/mo"

MUSIC: Subtle lo-fi tech pulse, 90 BPM, mixed at -18 dB under voiceover.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920, MP4, 30fps, H.264, under 30 seconds.
```
